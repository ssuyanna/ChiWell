所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：胸胁胀痛，脊背疼痛，呕吐，食不化，泄泻等。 (Chest and flank pain, spine pain, vomiting, inability to digest food, diarrhea, etc.)
位置：在背部，当第9胸椎棘突下，旁开3寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back, when the spinous process of the 9th thoracic vertebra is 3 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
