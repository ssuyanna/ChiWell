所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：下肢痿痹，足跟痛，脚气，腰痛，癫痫，踝关节炎等。 (Lower limb numbness, heel pain, athlete's foot, low back pain, epilepsy, ankle arthritis, etc.)
位置：在足外侧部，外踝后下方，昆仑直下，跟骨外侧，赤白肉际处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the lateral part of the foot, behind the lateral ankle, directly below Kunlun, on the lateral side of the calcaneal bone, at the red and white flesh.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
