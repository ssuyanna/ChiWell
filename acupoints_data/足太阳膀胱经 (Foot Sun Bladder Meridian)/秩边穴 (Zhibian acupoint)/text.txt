所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：腰骶痛，痔疾，下肢痿痹，及坐骨神经痛等。 (Lumbar sacral pain, hemorrhoids, lower limb numbness, and sciatica.)
位置：在臀部，平第4骶后孔，骶正中嵴旁开3寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the buttocks, level the 4th posterior sacral foramen, 3 inches away from the central sacral ridge.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
