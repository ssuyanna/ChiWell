所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：黄疸，口苦，呕吐，胁痛，惊悸，胸腹胀满，骨蒸潮热，肝火，胆囊炎，胃炎，肋间神经痛等。 (Jaundice, bitter mouth, vomiting, flank pain, palpitations, chest and abdomen bloating, bone steaming hot flashes, liver fire, cholecystitis, gastritis, intercostal neuralgia, etc.)
位置：在背部，当第十胸椎棘突下，旁开1.5寸处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back, below the spinous process of the tenth thoracic vertebra, 1.5 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
