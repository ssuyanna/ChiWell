所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：腰脊强痛，遗精，阳痿，小便不利，水肿，阴中肿痛，头昏目眩，耳鸣耳聋，月经不调，以及肾炎，肾盂肾炎，前列腺炎等。 (Strong pain in the waist and spine, nocturnal emission, impotence, difficulty urination, edema, swelling and pain in the vagina, dizziness, tinnitus and deafness, irregular menstruation, as well as nephritis, pyelonephritis, prostatitis, etc.)
位置：在腰部，当第2腰椎棘突下，旁开3寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the waist, when the spinous process of the second lumbar vertebra is 3 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
