所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽，胸闷，呕吐，失眠及风湿性心脏病，心动过速，心律不齐，心绞痛，肋间神经痛等。 (Cough, chest tightness, vomiting, insomnia and rheumatoid heart disease, tachycardia, arrhythmia, angina pectoris, intercostal neuralgia, etc.)
位置：在背部，当第4胸椎棘突下旁开1.5寸处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back, 1.5 inches below the spinous process of the 4th thoracic vertebra.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
