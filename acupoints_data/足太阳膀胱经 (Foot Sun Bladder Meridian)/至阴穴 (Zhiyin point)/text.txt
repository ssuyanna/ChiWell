所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，昏厥，鼻塞，目翳，滞产，胞衣不下，及胎位不正等。 (Headache, fainting, nasal congestion, eye cataracts, stagnation of birth, inability to lower the uterus, and improper fetal position.)
位置：在足小趾末节外侧，距趾甲角0.1寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the outside of the end of the little toe of the foot, 0.1 inch from the toenail angle.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
