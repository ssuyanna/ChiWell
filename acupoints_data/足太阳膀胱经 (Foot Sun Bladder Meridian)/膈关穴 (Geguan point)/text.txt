所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：呕吐，呃逆，噎膈，脊背疼痛，及肋间神经痛等。 (Vomiting, hiccups, choking, spinal pain, intercostal neuralgia, etc.)
位置：在背部，当第7胸椎棘突下，旁开3寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back, when the spinous process of the 7th thoracic vertebra is 3 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
