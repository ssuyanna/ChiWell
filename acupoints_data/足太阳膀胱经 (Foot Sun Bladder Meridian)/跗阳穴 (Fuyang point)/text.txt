所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：1. 运动系统疾病：急性腰扭伤，下肢瘫痪，腓肠肌痉挛，外踝红肿，脚气，脚腿痛，下肢痿痹等； 2. 精神神经系统疾病：面神经麻痹，三叉神经痛，头痛，头重等。 (1. Motor system diseases: acute lumbar sprain, lower limb paralysis, gastrocnemius spasm, redness and swelling of the lateral ankle, athlete's foot, foot and leg pain, lower limb paralysis, etc.; 2. Psychiatric nervous system diseases: facial nerve paralysis, trigeminal neuralgia, headache, heavy head, etc.)
位置：在小腿后面，外踝后，昆仑穴直上3寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Behind the calf and behind the outer ankle, the Kunlun point is 3 inches straight up.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
