所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：1. 精神神经系统疾病：臀股麻木，坐骨神经痛，下肢麻痹，小儿麻痹后遗症，瘫痪等； 2. 其它：腰背痛，急性腰部扭挫伤，股部炎症等。 (1. Mental and neurological diseases: numbness of hips, sciatica, lower limb paralysis, sequelae of polio, paralysis, etc.; 2. Others: low back pain, acute lumbar sprain and contusion, tribulation, etc.)
位置：在大腿后面，当承扶与委中的连线上，承扶下6寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Behind the thigh, on the connection between Chengfu and Wenzhong, Chengfu lowered 6 inches.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
