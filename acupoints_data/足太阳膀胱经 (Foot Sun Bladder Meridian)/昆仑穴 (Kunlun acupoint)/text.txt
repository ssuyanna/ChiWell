所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，目眩，项强，鼻衄，腰痛，脚跟痛，小儿癫痫，难产，胞衣不下，下肢麻痹或瘫痪，及坐骨神经痛，足踝关节及周围软组织疾患等。 (Headache, dizziness, neck strength, epilepsy, low back pain, heel pain, epilepsy in children, difficulty in delivery, inability to lower the cervical tract, paralysis or paralysis of the lower limbs, sciatica, foot and ankle joints and surrounding soft tissue diseases, etc.)
位置：在足部外踝后方，当外踝尖与跟腱之间的凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Behind the lateral ankle of the foot, the depression between the tip of the lateral ankle and the Achilles tendon.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
