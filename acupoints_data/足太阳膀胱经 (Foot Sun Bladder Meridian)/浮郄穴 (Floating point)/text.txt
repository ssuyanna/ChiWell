所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：便秘，股腘部疼痛，麻木。 (Constipation, femoropoplasty pain, numbness.)
位置：在腘横纹外侧端，委阳上1寸，股二头肌腱的内侧。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (At the lateral end of the popliteal transverse stripe, 1 inch above the bony, and the medial side of the biceps femoral tendon.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
