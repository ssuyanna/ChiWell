所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：1. 消化系统疾病：呕吐，反胃，腹胀，腹泻，消化性溃疡，胃炎，胃溃疡，胃扩张，胃下垂，胃痉挛，肝炎，腮腺炎，肠炎，痢疾； 2. 其它：糖尿病，失眠等。 (1. Digestive system diseases: vomiting, nausea, bloating, diarrhea, peptic ulcer, gastritis, gastric ulcer, gastric dilation, gastric sagging, gastric cramps, hepatitis, mumps, enteritis, dysentery; 2. Others: diabetes, insomnia, etc.)
位置：在背部，当第十二胸椎棘突下，旁开1.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back, when the spinous process of the twelfth thoracic vertebra is 1.5 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
