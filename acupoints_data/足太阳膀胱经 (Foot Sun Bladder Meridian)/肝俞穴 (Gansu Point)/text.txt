所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：1. 消化系统疾病：急慢性肝炎，胆囊炎，慢性胃炎，胃扩张，胃痉挛，黄疸； 2. 五官科系统疾病：眼睑下垂，结膜炎，青光眼，夜盲症，视网膜炎，黄褐斑、雀斑； 3. 精神神经系统疾病：偏头痛，神经衰弱，肋间神经痛，精神病； 4. 外科系统疾病：淋巴结结核，胃出血，肠出血，胆石症； 5. 其它：月经不调等。 (1. Digestive system diseases: acute and chronic hepatitis, cholecystitis, chronic gastritis, gastric dilation, gastric cramps, jaundice; 2. Oentreological system diseases: ptosis, conjunctivitis, glaucoma, nocturnal blindness, retinitis, chloasma, freckles; 3. Mental and neurological diseases: migraine, neurasthenia, intercostal neuralgia, psychiatric disease; 4. Surgical system diseases: lymph node tuberculosis, gastric bleeding, intestinal bleeding, cholelithiasis; 5. Others: irregular menstruation, etc.)
位置：在背部，当第9胸椎棘突下，旁开1.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back, when the spinous process of the 9th thoracic vertebra is 1.5 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
