所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：1. 消化系统疾病：神经性呕吐，胃炎，胃溃疡，肝炎，肠炎，肠出血； 2. 循环系统疾病：心动过速，心脏肥大，心内外膜炎； 3. 外科系统疾病：食道癌，胃癌，食道狭窄，淋巴结结核，胸膜炎； 4. 呼吸系统疾病：哮喘，支气管炎； 5. 其它：贫血，慢性出血性疾患，膈肌痉挛，荨麻疹，小儿营养不良。 (1. Digestive system diseases: neurological vomiting, gastritis, gastric ulcer, hepatitis, enteritis, intestinal bleeding; 2. Circulatory system diseases: tachycardia, cardiac hypertrophy, internal and external meridianitis; 3. Surgical system diseases: esophageal cancer, gastric cancer, esophageal stenosis, lymph node tuberculosis, pleurisitis; 4. Respiratory system diseases: asthma, bronchitis; 5. Others: anemia, chronic hemorrhagic diseases, diaphragm spasm, urticaria, and malnutrition in children.)
位置：在背中，当第7胸椎棘突下，旁开1.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the back, when the spinous process of the 7th thoracic vertebra is 1.5 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
