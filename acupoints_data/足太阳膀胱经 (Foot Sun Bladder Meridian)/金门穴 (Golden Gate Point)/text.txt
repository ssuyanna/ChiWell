所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，眩晕，癫痫，小儿惊风，腰膝痛，下肢痿痹，外踝痛。 (Headache, dizziness, epilepsy, convulsions in children, waist and knee pain, lower limb numbness, and external ankle pain.)
位置：在足外侧，当外踝前缘直下，骰骨下缘处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the outside of the foot, when the anterior edge of the lateral ankle is straight down, at the lower edge of the cubic bone.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
