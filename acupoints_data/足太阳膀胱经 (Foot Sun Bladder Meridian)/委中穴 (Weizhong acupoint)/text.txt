所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：1. 坐骨神经痛、腰背痛、小腿疲劳、臀部疼痛、膝盖疼痛、下肢痿痹等腰及下肢病证； 2. 腹痛，急性吐泻； 3. 小便不利，遗尿； 4. 丹毒。 (1. Sciatica, low back pain, calf fatigue, buttock pain, knee pain, lower limb paralysis and other symptoms of lower limbs; 2. Abdominal pain, acute vomiting and diarrhea; 3. Difficulty in urination, enuresis; 4. Essay.)
位置：位于人体的腘横纹中点，当股二头肌腱与半腱肌肌腱的中间。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located at the midpoint of the popliteal transverse striae of the human body, between the biceps femoral tendon and the semi-tendental tendon.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
