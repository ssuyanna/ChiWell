所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：肠鸣腹胀，腰痛，月经不调，痛经，痔漏下血，下肢瘫痪等。 (Intestinal rumbling and abdominal distension, low back pain, irregular menstruation, dysmenorrhea, bleeding from hemorrhoids, paralysis of the lower limbs, etc.)
位置：在腰部，当第3腰椎棘突下，旁开1.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the waist, when the spinous process of the third lumbar vertebra is 1.5 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
