所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽，气喘，疟疾，热病，肩背痛。 (Cough, asthma, malaria, fever, shoulder and back pain.)
位置：在背部，当第6胸椎棘突下，旁开3寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back, when the spinous process of the 6th thoracic vertebra is 3 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
