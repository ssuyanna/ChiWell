所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：肺经及呼吸道疾病，如咳嗽，气喘，咯血，骨蒸潮热，盗汗，及支气管炎，支气管哮喘，肺炎，肺结核，荨麻疹，皮肤瘙痒症等。 (Lung meridian and respiratory diseases, such as cough, asthma, hemoptysis, bone steaming hot flashes, night sweats, and bronchitis, bronchial asthma, pneumonia, tuberculosis, urticaria, skin itching, etc.)
位置：在背部，当第3胸椎棘突下，旁开1.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back, when the spinous process of the third thoracic vertebra is 1.5 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
