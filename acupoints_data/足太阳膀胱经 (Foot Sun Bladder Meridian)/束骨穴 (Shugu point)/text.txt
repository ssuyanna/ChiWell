所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，目眩，项强，癫、狂、痫，腰背痛等。 (Headache, dizziness, neck strength, epilepsy, madness, epilepsy, low back pain, etc.)
位置：在足外侧，足小趾本节（第5跖趾关节）的后方，赤白肉际处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the lateral side of the foot, behind this section of the little toe of the foot (5th metatarsophalal joint), at the red and white fleshy ends.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
