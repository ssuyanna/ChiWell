所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，目眩，项强，癫狂，鼻衄等。 (Headache, dizziness, neck strength, madness, nosebleed, etc.)
位置：在足外侧，足小趾本节（第5跖趾关节）的前方，赤白肉际处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the lateral side of the foot, in front of this section of the little toe of the foot (5th metatarsophalal joint), at the red and white flesh.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
