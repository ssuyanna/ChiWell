所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：腰骶疼痛，月经不调，赤白带下，阴挺，痛经，疝气，下肢痿痹，及坐骨神经痛，盆腔炎等。 (Lumbar sacral pain, irregular menstruation, red and leucorrhea, yin protrusion, dysmenorrhea, hernia, lower limb numbness, sciatica, pelvic inflammatory disease, etc.)
位置：在骶部，当髂后上棘内下方，适对第二骶后孔处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the sacral part, when the posterior superior iliac spine is below, it is suitable for the second posterior posterior sacral foramen.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
