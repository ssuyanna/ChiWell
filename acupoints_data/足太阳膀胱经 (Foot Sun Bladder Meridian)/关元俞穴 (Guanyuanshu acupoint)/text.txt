所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：腰痛，腹胀，泄泻，痢疾，遗尿，小便频数或不利，消渴及膀胱炎等。 (Low back pain, abdominal distension, diarrhea, dysentery, enuresis, frequent or disadvantage of urination, diabetes and cystitis, etc.)
位置：在腰部，当第5腰椎棘突下，旁开1.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the waist, when the spinous process of the 5th lumbar vertebra is 1.5 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
