所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，项强，癫痫，腰腿痛，踝关节痛等。 (Headache, neck strength, epilepsy, lower back and leg pain, ankle pain, etc.)
位置：在足外侧，第5跖骨粗隆下方，赤白肉际处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the lateral side of the foot, below the trochondrature of the 5th metatarsal, at the red and white flesh.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
