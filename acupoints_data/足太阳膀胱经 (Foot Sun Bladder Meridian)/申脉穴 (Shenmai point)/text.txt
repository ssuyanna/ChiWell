所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，眩晕，癫、狂、痫，腰腿痛，足踝关节痛，怕冷症（怯寒症）等。 (Headache, dizziness, epilepsy, madness, epilepsy, lower back and leg pain, foot and ankle joint pain, cold fear (cold timidity), etc.)
位置：在足外侧部，外踝直下方凹陷中。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the lateral side of the foot, the lateral ankle is sunken directly below.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
