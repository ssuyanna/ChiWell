所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽，气喘，肺痨，健忘，遗精，完谷不化。 (Cough, asthma, tuberculosis, forgetfulness, nocturnal emission, and the grain will not melt.)
位置：位于人体的背部，当第四胸椎棘突下，左右四指宽处（或旁开三寸），肩胛骨内侧，一压即疼。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located on the back of the human body, it hurts when it is under the spinous process of the fourth thoracic vertebra, at the width of the left and right four fingers (or three inches apart), and on the inner side of the scapula after pressing.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
