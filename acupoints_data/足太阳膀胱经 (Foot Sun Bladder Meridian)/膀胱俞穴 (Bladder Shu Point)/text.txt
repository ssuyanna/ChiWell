所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：小便不利，遗尿，泄泻，便秘，腰脊强痛。 (Difficulty in urination, enuresis, diarrhea, constipation, and strong pain in the back and spine.)
位置：在骶部，当骶正中嵴旁1.5寸，平第2骶后孔。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the sacral part, 1.5 inches next to the central sacral ridge, level the second posterior sacral foramen.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
