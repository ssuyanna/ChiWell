所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：腰背痛，腓肠肌痉挛，小腹胀满，小便不利等。 (Low back pain, gastrocnemius spasm, lower abdomen fullness, difficulty urinating, etc.)
位置：在腘窝纹外侧端，当股二头肌腱的内侧。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (At the lateral end of the popliteal striatum, the medial side of the biceps femoral tendon.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
