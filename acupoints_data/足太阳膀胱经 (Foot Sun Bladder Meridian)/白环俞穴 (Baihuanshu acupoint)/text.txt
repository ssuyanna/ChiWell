所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：遗精，疝气，白带，血崩，大小便不利，腰髋痛，及坐骨神经痛，下肢瘫痪，子宫内膜炎，肛门诸肌痉挛，小儿麻痹后遗症，下肢瘫痪，尿潴留等。 (Nocturnal emission, hernia, leucorrhea, hemocystic, urinary and defecation, lumbar and hip pain, sciatica, lower limb paralysis, endometritis, anal muscle spasm, sequelae of polio, lower limb paralysis, urinary retention, etc.)
位置：在骶部，当骶正中嵴旁1.5寸，平第四骶后孔。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the sacral part, 1.5 inches next to the central sacral ridge, level the fourth posterior sacral foramen.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
