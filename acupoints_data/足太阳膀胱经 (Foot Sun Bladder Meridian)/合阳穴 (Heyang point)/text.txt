所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：1. 妇产科系统疾病：功能性子宫出血，月经不调，子宫内膜炎； 2. 泌尿生殖系统疾病：睾丸炎，前列腺炎； 3. 其它：脑血管病后遗症，肠出血，疝痛，腓肠肌痉挛。 (1. Obstetric and gynecological system diseases: functional uterine bleeding, irregular menstruation, endometriitis; 2. Urogenital system diseases: orchitis, prostatitis; 3. Others: sequelae of cerebrovascular disease, intestinal bleeding, hernia pain, gastrocnemius spasm.)
位置：在小腿后面，当委中与承山的连线上，委中下2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Behind the calf, on the connection between Wenzhong and Chengshan, Wenzhong is 2 inches lower.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
