所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：便秘，泄泻，小便不利，月经不调，带下，腰痛。 (Constipation, diarrhea, difficulty in urination, irregular menstruation, lower back pain.)
位置：在骶部，当次髎下内方，适对第3骶后孔处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the sacral part, when the inner side is below the second sacral part, it is appropriate to the third posterior sacral foramen.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
