所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，目眩，鼻塞，热病。面神经麻痹，角膜白斑，鼻息肉，鼻炎，内耳眩晕症等。 (Headache, dizziness, nasal congestion, fever.Facial nerve paralysis, corneal leukoplakia, nasal polyps, rhinitis, inner ear vertigo, etc.)
位置：在头部，当前发际正中直上2.5寸，旁开1.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the head, the hairline is 2.5 inches straight up in the middle and 1.5 inches away.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
