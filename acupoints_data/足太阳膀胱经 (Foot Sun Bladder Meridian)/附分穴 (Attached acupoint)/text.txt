所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：1. 运动系统疾病：颈椎病，颈部肌肉痉挛，肩背拘紧，颈项强痛，肘臂麻木； 2. 精神神经系统疾病：肋间神经痛，副神经麻痹； 3. 其它：肺炎，感冒。 (1. Motor system diseases: cervical spondylosis, neck muscle spasm, shoulder tightness, neck pain, elbow and arm numbness; 2. Mental neurological diseases: intercostal neuralgia, paralysis of the paranormal nerve; 3. Others: pneumonia, cold.)
位置：在背部，当第2胸椎棘突下，旁开3寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back, when the spinous process of the second thoracic vertebra is 3 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
