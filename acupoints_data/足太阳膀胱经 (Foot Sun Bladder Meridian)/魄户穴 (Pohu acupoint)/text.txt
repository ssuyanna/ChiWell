所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽，气喘，虚劳，颈项强痛，肩背痛等。 (Cough, asthma, deficiency, strong neck pain, shoulder and back pain, etc.)
位置：在背部，当第3胸椎棘突下，旁开3寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back, when the spinous process of the third thoracic vertebra is 3 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
