所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：1. 腰腿拘急、疼痛； 2. 痔疾，便秘。 (1. Emergency and pain in the waist and legs; 2. Hemorrhoids and constipation.)
位置：在小腿后面正中，委中穴与昆仑穴之间，当伸直小腿和足跟上提时腓肠肌肌腹下出现凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the middle of the back of the calf, between Weizhong point and Kunlun point, a depression appears under the abdomen of the gastrocnemius muscle when the calf and heel are straightened.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
