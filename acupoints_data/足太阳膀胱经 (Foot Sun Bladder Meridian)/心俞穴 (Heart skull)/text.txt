所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：心经及循环系统疾病，心痛，惊悸，咳嗽，吐血，失眠，健忘，盗汗，梦遗，癫痫，胸痛，心悸亢进，晕车，头痛，恶心想吐，风湿性心脏病，冠心病，心动过速或过缓，神经官能症等。 (Heart meridian and circulatory system diseases, heartache, palpitations, cough, vomiting of blood, insomnia, forgetfulness, night sweats, nocturnal emissions, epilepsy, chest pain, hyperpnea, motion sickness, headache, nausea and vomiting, rheumatic heart disease, coronary heart disease, tachycardia or hypersurgery, neurosis, etc.)
位置：在背部，当第5胸椎棘突下，旁开1.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back, when the spinous process of the 5th thoracic vertebra is 1.5 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
