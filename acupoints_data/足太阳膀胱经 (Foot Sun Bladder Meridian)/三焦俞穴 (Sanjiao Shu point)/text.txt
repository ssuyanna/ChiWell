所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：腹胀，肠鸣，呕吐，泄痢，水肿，鼓胀，黄疸，遗尿，癃闭，腰脊强痛等。 (Abdominal bloating, intestinal rumbling, vomiting, diarrhea, edema, swelling, jaundice, enuresis, closure, strong pain in the waist and spine.)
位置：在腰部，当第1腰椎棘突下，旁开1.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the waist, when the spinous process of the first lumbar vertebra is 1.5 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
