所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：腹痛、泄泻，痢疾，便血，痔疮，带下，阳痿，阴部湿痒等。 (Abdominal pain, diarrhea, dysentery, bloody stools, hemorrhoids, underwear, impotence, vaginal dampness and itching, etc.)
位置：在骶部，尾骨端旁开0.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the sacral part, 0.5 inches away from the coccyx end.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
