所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：下腹部肿胀、下腹部疼痛、脚部肿胀、夜尿症，小腹胀痛，赤白痢疾，遗精，遗尿，尿血，疝气，赤白带下，腰骶疼痛，及盆腔炎等。此外此穴和大肠俞穴配合可以明显改善男性早泄状况。 (Swelling of the lower abdomen, pain in the lower abdomen, swelling of the feet, nocturnal dysentery, red and white dysentery, nocturnal sperm, enuresis, hematuria, hernia, red and leucorrhea, lumbar sacral pain, and pelvic inflammatory disease.In addition, this point and the Datang Shu point can significantly improve men's premature ejaculation.)
位置：在骶部，当骶正中嵴旁1.5寸，平第一骶后孔。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the sacral part, 1.5 inches next to the central sacral ridge, flatten the first posterior sacral foramen.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
