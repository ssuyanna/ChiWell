所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，目赤，鼻塞，眩晕，癫痫等。 (Headache, red eyes, congestion in the nose, dizziness, epilepsy, etc.)
位置：位于人体的头部，当攒竹穴直上入发际0.5寸，神庭穴与曲差穴连线之间。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located on the head of the human body, when the Zanzhu point goes straight up to the hairline 0.5 inches, between the connection between the Shenting point and the Qucai point.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
