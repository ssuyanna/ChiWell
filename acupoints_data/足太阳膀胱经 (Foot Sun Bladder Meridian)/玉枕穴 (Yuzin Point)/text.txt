所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：头项痛，目痛，鼻塞。 (Headache, eye pain, nasal congestion.)
位置：在后头部，当后发际正中直上2.5寸，旁开1.3寸，平枕外隆凸上缘的凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the posterior head, when the posterior hairline is straight up 2.5 inches and 1.3 inches away, the depression on the upper edge of the cushion is flat.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
