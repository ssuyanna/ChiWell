所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：腰骶疼痛，月经不调，赤白带下，大小便不利，下肢痿痹等。 (Lumbar sacral pain, irregular menstruation, red and leucorrhea, difficulty in urination and defecation, lower limb weakness, etc.)
位置：在骶部，当髂后上棘与后正中线之间，适对第一骶后孔处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the sacral part, the posterior iliac spine and the posterior median line is appropriate to the first posterior sacral foramen.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
