所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：脘腹胀痛，肠鸣泄泻，饮食不下，黄疸，消渴，肝炎，胆囊炎，胆道蛔虫症等。 (Abdominal distension and pain, intestinal rumbling and diarrhea, inability to eat, jaundice, diabetes, hepatitis, cholecystitis, biliary ascariasis, etc.)
位置：在背部，当第10胸椎棘突下，旁开3寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back, when the spinous process of the 10th thoracic vertebra is 3 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
