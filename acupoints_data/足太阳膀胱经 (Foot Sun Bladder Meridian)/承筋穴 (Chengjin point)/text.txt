所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：小腿痛，腓肠肌痉挛，腰背痛，痔疮。 (Calf pain, gastrocnemius spasm, low back pain, hemorrhoids.)
位置：在小腿后面，当委中与承山连线上，腓肠股肌肌腹中央，委中下5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Behind the calf, on the connection between Weizhong and Chengshan, the center of the gastrocnemius femoral muscle, 5 inches below Weizhong.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
