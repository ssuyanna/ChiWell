所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：感冒，发热，头痛，咳嗽，喘息，项强，肩背痛等。 (Cold, fever, headache, cough, wheezing, neck strength, shoulder and back pain, etc.)
位置：在背部，当第1胸椎棘突下，旁开1.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back, when the spinous process of the first thoracic vertebra is 1.5 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
