所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：腹泻，痢疾，疝气，腰骶痛，坐骨神经痛等。 (Diarrhea, dysentery, hernia, lumbosacral pain, sciatica, etc.)
位置：在骶部，当骶正中嵴旁1.5寸，平第三骶后孔。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the sacral part, when the central sacral ridge is 1.5 inches away from the third posacral foramen.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
