所属经脉：足太阳膀胱经 (Foot Sun Bladder Meridian)
主调病症：恶心、呕吐、腹胀、肠鸣、泄泻、黄疸、饮食不下等。 (Nausea, vomiting, abdominal distension, intestinal rumbling, diarrhea, jaundice, and poor diet.)
位置：在背部，当第11胸椎棘突下，旁开3寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back, when the spinous process of the 11th thoracic vertebra is 3 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
