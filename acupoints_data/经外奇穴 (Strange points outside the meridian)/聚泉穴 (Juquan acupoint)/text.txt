所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：舌强，舌缓，舌肌麻痹，味觉减退，食不知味，久咳不愈，支气管哮喘，消渴。 (The tongue is strong, the tongue is slow, the tongue muscles are paralyzed, the sense of taste is reduced, the food is not sensible, the long-term cough cannot heal, bronchial asthma, and thirst.)
位置：位于口腔内，当舌背正中缝的中点处。 (Located in the mouth, at the midpoint of the mid-slit of the dorsal joint of the tongue.)
