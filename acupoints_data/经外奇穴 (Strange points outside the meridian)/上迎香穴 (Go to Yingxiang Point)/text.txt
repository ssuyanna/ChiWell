所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：单纯性鼻炎，过敏性鼻炎，肥大性鼻炎，萎缩性鼻炎，鼻旁窦炎，鼻衄，鼻息肉，嗅觉功能障碍；慢性结膜炎，烂眼弦，暴发火眼，迎风流泪，泪囊炎；感冒，头痛，鼻塞；口眼歪斜，头面疔疮。 (Simple rhinitis, allergic rhinitis, hypertrophic rhinitis, atrophic rhinitis, paranormal sinusitis, epistaxis, nasal polyps, olfactory dysfunction; chronic conjunctivitis, rotten eye strings, ignorant eyes, tears in the wind, tear cystitis; colds, headaches, nasal congestion; crooked mouth and eyes, head and face boils.)
位置：在面部，当鼻翼软骨与鼻甲的交界处，近鼻唇沟上端处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the face, at the junction of the nasal cartilage and the turbinate, near the upper end of the nasal labial groove.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
