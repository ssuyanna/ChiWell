所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：1. 目赤肿痛，眼睑下垂，近视，急性结膜炎； 2. 面神经麻痹； 3. 三叉神经痛。 (1. Red and swollen eyes, ptosis, myopia, acute conjunctivitis; 2. Facial nerve paralysis; 3. Trigeminal neuralgia.)
位置：位于额部，瞳孔直上，眉毛中。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located on the forehead, the pupils are straight up, and in the eyebrows.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
