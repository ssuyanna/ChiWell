所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：面神经麻痹、三叉神经痛、面肌痉挛等。 (Facial nerve paralysis, trigeminal neuralgia, facial muscle spasm, etc.)
位置：在面部承浆穴外侧约1寸之凹陷中。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In a depression about 1 inch outside the face of Chengji Point.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
