所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：胆囊炎，胆石症，胆道蛔虫症，胆绞痛等胆道疾病；腰腿痛，下肢痿痹；胸胁痛，慢性胃炎；口眼歪斜。 (Cholecystitis, cholelithiasis, biliary ascariasis, biliary colic and other biliary diseases; lumbar and leg pain, lower limb paralysis; chest and flank pain, chronic gastritis; oral and eye crooked.)
位置：在小腿外侧上部，当腓骨小头前下方凹陷处，胆经阳陵泉穴直下1~2寸压痛处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the upper part of the lateral calves, in the depression in front of the small head of the fibula, the tenderness point 1 to 2 inches down the Yanglingquan point of the gallbladder meridian.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
