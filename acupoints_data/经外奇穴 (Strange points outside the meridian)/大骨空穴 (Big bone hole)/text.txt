所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：目痛，翳膜内障，鼻衄，吐泻等。 (Eye pain, cataracts, epistaxis, vomiting and diarrhea, etc.)
位置：在拇指背侧指间关节的中点处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (At the midpoint of the interphalangeal joint on the dorsal side of the thumb.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
