所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：1. 偏、正头痛，神经性头痛，眩晕； 2. 目赤肿痛，鼻炎。 (1. Negative and positive headache, neurological headache, dizziness; 2. Red, swollen and painful eyes, rhinitis.)
位置：在头前部，当瞳孔直上，前发际上1寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the front of the head, when the pupil is straight up, 1 inch above the anterior hairline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
