所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：牙痛，乳蛾；小儿不语；霍乱；转筋。 (Toothache, wet moth; children are silent; cholera; tendons turn.)
位置：在足内侧面，内踝凸起处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the medial side of the foot, at the protrusion of the medial ankle.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
