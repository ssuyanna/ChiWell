所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：脚气，足趾麻痹，足背红肿，并用于急救。 (Athletic foot, numb toes, redness and swelling of the back of the foot, and is used for first aid.)
位置：在足十趾尖端，距趾甲游离缘0.1寸，左右共十穴。 (At the tip of the ten toes of the foot, 0.1 inch away from the free edge of the toenail, there are ten points on the left and right.)
