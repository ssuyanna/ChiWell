所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：膝关节痛，中风偏瘫，腿疼痛无力，膝部红肿。 (Knee joint pain, stroke hemiplegia, leg pain and weakness, and redness and swollen knees.)
位置：在大腿前面下部，当胃经梁丘穴两旁各1.5寸处，一侧两穴，左右共4穴。 (In the lower front of the thigh, at 1.5 inches on both sides of Liangqiu point in the stomach meridian, there are two points on one side, and 4 points on the left and right.)
