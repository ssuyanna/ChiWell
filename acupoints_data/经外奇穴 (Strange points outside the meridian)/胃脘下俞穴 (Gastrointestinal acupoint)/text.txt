所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：胃痛，腹痛，胸胁痛，消渴，咳嗽，咽干，呕吐，胰腺炎，糖尿病。 (Stomach pain, abdominal pain, chest and flank pain, diabetes, cough, dry throat, vomiting, pancreatitis, diabetes.)
位置：在背部，当第8胸椎棘突下，旁开1.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back, when the spinous process of the 8th thoracic vertebra is 1.5 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
