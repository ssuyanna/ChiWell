所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：腹痛，泄泻，腰痛；膀胱炎，肠炎等；小便不利，遗尿。 (Abdominal pain, diarrhea, low back pain; cystitis, enteritis, etc.; difficulty in urination, enuresis.)
位置：在腰部，当后正中线上，第三腰椎棘突下。 (On the waist, on the posterior median line, under the spinous process of the third lumbar vertebra.)
