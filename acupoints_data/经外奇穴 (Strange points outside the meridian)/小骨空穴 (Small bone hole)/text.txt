所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：1. 眼病，咽喉炎； 2. 掌指关节痛。 (1. Eye disease, pharyngitis; 2. Metachondral pain.)
位置：在小指背侧，近侧指间关节的中点处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the dorsal side of the little finger, at the midpoint of the proximal interphalangeal joint.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
