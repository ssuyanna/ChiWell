所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：胃疼，腹痛，胸胁痛，消渴，胰腺炎等。 (Stomach pain, abdominal pain, chest and flank pain, diabetes, pancreatitis, etc.)
位置：在第8胸椎棘突下旁开1.5寸，膈俞穴与肝俞穴之间。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (1.5 inches away from the spinous process of the 8th thoracic vertebra, between the geshu point and the liver point.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
