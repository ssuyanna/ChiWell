所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：1. 头痛，头晕； 2. 鼻炎，目赤肿痛，三叉神经痛； 3. 失眠，高血压，目眩，眼部疾病等。 (1. Headache, dizziness; 2. Rhinitis, red, swollen and painful eyes, trigeminal neuralgia; 3. Insomnia, hypertension, dizziness, eye diseases, etc.)
位置：位于前额部，当两眉头间连线与前正中线之交点处。 (Located on the forehead, at the intersection of the line between the two eyebrows and the front center.)
