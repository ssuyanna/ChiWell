所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：腰部软组织损伤，腰痛，妇人血崩，脊柱肌痉挛。 (Soft tissue injury in the waist, low back pain, hemorrhage in women, and spinal muscle spasm.)
位置：在腰部，当第4腰椎棘突下，后正中线旁开3寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (At the waist, when the spinous process of the fourth lumbar vertebra is 3 inches away from the posterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
