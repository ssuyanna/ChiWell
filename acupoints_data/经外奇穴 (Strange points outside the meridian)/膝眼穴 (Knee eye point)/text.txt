所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：膝关节酸痛，鹤膝风，腿痛及其周围软组织炎。 (Knee joint soreness, knee wind, leg pain and surrounding soft tissue inflammation.)
位置：在髌韧带两侧凹陷处；内侧的称内膝眼，外侧的称外膝眼。左右各2穴，共4穴。 (In the depressions on both sides of the patellar ligament; the inner side is called the inner knee eye, and the outer side is called the outer knee eye.There are 2 points on the left and right, and 4 points in total.)
