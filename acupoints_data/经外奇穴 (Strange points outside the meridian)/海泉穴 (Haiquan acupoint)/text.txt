所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：舌缓不收，重舌肿胀，喉痹，高热，单乳蛾，呕吐，呃逆，腹泻，腹痛，消渴。 (The tongue is slow and does not retract, severe tongue swelling, throat numbness, high fever, single moth, vomiting, hiccups, diarrhea, abdominal pain, and diabetes.)
位置：位于口腔内，当舌下系带中点处。 (Located in the mouth, at the midpoint of the sublingual lace.)
