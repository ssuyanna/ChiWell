所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：1. 舌强，舌肿，口疮，喉闭； 2. 呕吐，消渴，腹泻； 3.失语。 (1. Strong tongue, swollen tongue, mouth sores, and throat closure; 2. Vomiting, thirst, diarrhea; 3. Aphasia.)
位置：位于口腔内，舌下系带两旁之静脉上。左侧为金津，右侧为玉液。 (Located in the oral cavity, on the veins on both sides of the sublingual lace.The left side is gold and the right side is jade liquid.)
