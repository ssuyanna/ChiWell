所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：头痛，眩晕，失眠，健忘，癫痫；脑血管病，高血压，神经衰弱，精神病，小儿多动症，血管性痴呆，大脑发育不全等。 (Headache, dizziness, insomnia, forgetfulness, epilepsy; cerebrovascular disease, hypertension, neurasthenia, psychosis, pediatric ADHD, vascular dementia, brain insufficiency, etc.)
位置：百会穴前后左右各1寸，共4穴。 (Baihui point is 1 inch in front, back, left and right, and a total of 4 points.)
