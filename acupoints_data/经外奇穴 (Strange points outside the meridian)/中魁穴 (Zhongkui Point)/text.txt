所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：噎膈，呕吐，食欲不振，呃逆。 (Choking, vomiting, loss of appetite, and hiccups.)
位置：在中指背侧近侧侧指间关节的中点处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (At the midpoint of the interphalangeal joint on the dorsal proximal middle finger.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
