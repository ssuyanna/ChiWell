所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：皮肤瘙痒症，荨麻疹，风湿痒疹，阴囊湿疹，下部生疮，蛔虫病，膝关节病，肾脏风疮，产后风。 (Pruritus of the skin, urticaria, rheumatoid eczema, scrotal eczema, lower sores, ascariasis, knee arthritis, kidney wind sores, postpartum wind.)
位置：在大腿内侧，髌底内侧端上3寸，即脾经血海穴上1寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the inner thigh, 3 inches above the inner end of the patellar base, that is, 1 inch above the Xuehai point of the spleen meridian.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
