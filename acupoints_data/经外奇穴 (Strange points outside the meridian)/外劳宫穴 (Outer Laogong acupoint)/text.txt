所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：落枕；手臂肿痛；脐风。 (Stiff pillow; swollen and painful arms; umbilical wind.)
位置：左手背侧，当第2、第3掌骨间，指掌关节后约0.5寸处（指寸）。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the dorsal side of the left hand, between the second and third metacarpal bones, about 0.5 inches behind the finger joint (referring to inches).(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
