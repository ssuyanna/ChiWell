所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：1. 目赤肿痛，急性结膜炎，角膜炎； 2. 偏正头痛。 (1. Red, swollen and painful eyes, acute conjunctivitis, keratitis; 2. Miorrectile headache.)
位置：在耳廓的上方，当折耳向前，耳廓上方的尖端处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Above the auricle, when the folded ears are forward, at the tip above the auricle.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
