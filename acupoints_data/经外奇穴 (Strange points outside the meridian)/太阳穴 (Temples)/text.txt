所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：头痛，偏头痛，感冒，眩晕，牙痛，目赤肿痛，及三叉神经痛，面神经麻痹，急性结膜炎，麦粒肿等。 (Headache, migraine, cold, dizziness, toothache, red and swollen eyes, trigeminal neuralgia, facial nerve paralysis, acute conjunctivitis, sty, etc.)
位置：在耳廓前面，前额两侧，外眼角延长线的上方， 两眉梢后凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In front of the auricle, on both sides of the forehead, above the extension line of the outer corners of the eyebrow, the depression behind the tips of the eyebrows.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
