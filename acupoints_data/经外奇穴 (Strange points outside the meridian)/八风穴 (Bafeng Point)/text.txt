所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：1. 牙痛，胃痛，足跗肿痛； 2. 月经不调等。 (1. Toothache, stomach pain, swollen and painful foot tars; 2. Irregular menstruation, etc.)
位置：在足背侧，第一至五趾间，趾蹼缘后方赤白肉际处，一侧四穴，左右共八个穴位。 (On the dorsal side of the foot, between the first to fifth toes, at the red and white flesh at the back of the webbing of the toe, there are four acupoints on one side, and eight acupoints on the left and right.)
