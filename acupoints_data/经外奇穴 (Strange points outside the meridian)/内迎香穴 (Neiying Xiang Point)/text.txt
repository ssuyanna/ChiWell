所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：目赤肿痛，鼻疾，喉痹，热病，中暑，眩晕。 (Red, swollen and painful eyes, nasal disease, throat numbness, fever, heat stroke, and dizziness.)
位置：在鼻孔，当鼻翼软骨与鼻甲的粘膜处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the nostrils, when the nasal cartilage and the mucosa of the nasal turbinate.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
