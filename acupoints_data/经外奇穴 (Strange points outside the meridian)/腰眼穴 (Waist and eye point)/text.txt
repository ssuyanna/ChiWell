所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：月经不调，带下，子宫内膜炎；腰痛，腰肌纤维炎，腰肌劳损。 (Irregular menstruation, lowering, endometriitis; low back pain, lumbar muscle fibritis, lumbar muscle strain.)
位置：第4腰椎棘突下，旁开约3.5寸凹陷中。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Under the spinous process of the 4th lumbar vertebra, the side is about 3.5 inches of depression.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
