所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：1. 阑尾炎，消化不良； 2. 下肢痿痹。 (1. Appendicitis, indigestion; 2. Lower limb numbness.)
位置：足三里穴直下2寸。膝膑以下约五寸，胫骨前嵴外侧一横指处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Zusanli point is 2 inches down.About five inches below the knee patella, at a horizontal finger on the outer side of the anterior ridge of the tibia.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
