所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：1. 月经不调； 2. 心绞痛，胃痛，呕吐。 (1. Irregular menstruation; 2. Angina, stomach pain, vomiting.)
位置：在足第二趾的跖侧远侧趾间关节的中点。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (At the midpoint of the interphalangeal joint on the second toe of the foot.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
