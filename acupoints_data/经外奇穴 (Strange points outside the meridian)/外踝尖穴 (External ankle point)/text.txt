所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：脚趾拘急，踝关节肿痛；脚气；牙痛。 (Toes are tight, swollen and painful ankle joints; athlete's foot; toothache.)
位置：在足外侧面，外踝凸起处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the lateral side of the foot, at the bulge of the outer ankle.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
