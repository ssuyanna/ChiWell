所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：1. 痔疮，脱肛； 2. 前臂痛； 3. 胸肋痛。 (1. Hemorrhoids, prolapse of the anus; 2. Forearm pain; 3. Chest and rib pain.)
位置：前臂掌面，腕横纹上四寸，桡侧腕屈肌腱两侧，各一穴，左右共4穴。 (On the palm of the forearm, four inches above the horizontal line of the wrist, one point on both sides of the radial flexor wrist tendon, and four points on the left and right.)
