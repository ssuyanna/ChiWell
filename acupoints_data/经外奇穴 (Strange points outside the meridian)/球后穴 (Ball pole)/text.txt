所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：视神经萎缩，视神经炎，视网膜色素变性，视网膜动脉或静脉阻塞，中心性视网膜病变，色觉异常，近视，青光眼，早期白内障，玻璃体混浊，内斜视等等。 (Optic atrophy, optic neuritis, retinal pigmentation, retinal artery or venous obstruction, central retinopathy, color abnormality, myopia, glaucoma, early cataracts, vitreous opacity, endostrils, etc.)
位置：在面部，当眶下缘的外1/4与内3/4交界处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the face, at the junction of the outer 1/4 and the inner 3/4 of the infraorbital edge.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
