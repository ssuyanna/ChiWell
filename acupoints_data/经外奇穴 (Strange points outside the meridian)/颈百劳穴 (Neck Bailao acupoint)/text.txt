所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：1. 咳嗽，哮喘，肺结核； 2. 颈项强痛，角弓反张。 (1. Cough, asthma, tuberculosis; 2. Strong neck pain, reverse horn arch.)
位置：位于项部，当大椎穴直上2寸，后正中线旁开1寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located at the neck, 2 inches up the Dazhui point and 1 inch away from the back midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
