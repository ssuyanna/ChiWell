所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：子宫下垂，月经不调，痛经，功能性子宫出血，子宫内膜炎，不孕症，阑尾炎，盆腔炎，睾丸炎等。 (Uterine sagging, irregular menstruation, dysmenorrhea, functional uterine bleeding, endometritis, infertility, appendicitis, pelvic inflammatory disease, orchitis, etc.)
位置：在下腹部，当脐中下4寸，中极旁开3寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the lower abdomen, 4 inches below the navel, 3 inches away from the middle pole.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
