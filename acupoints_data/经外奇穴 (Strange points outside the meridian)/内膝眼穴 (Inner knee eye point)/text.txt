所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：膝关节酸痛，鹤膝风，腿痛及其周围软组织炎。 (Knee joint soreness, knee wind, leg pain and surrounding soft tissue inflammation.)
位置：在髌韧带内侧凹陷处内侧。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Medial on the depression on the medial side of the patellar ligament.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
