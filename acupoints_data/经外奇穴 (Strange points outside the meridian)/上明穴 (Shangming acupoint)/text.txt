所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：屈光不正，青光眼，白内障、近视、复视、迎风流泪、眼底出血、上睑下垂、视神经萎缩、面瘫等。 (Refractive error, glaucoma, cataract, myopia, diplopia, tears in the wind, fundus hemorrhage, ptosis, optic atrophy, facial paralysis, etc.)
位置：在额部，眉弓中点垂线，眶上缘下凹陷中。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the forehead, the vertical line of the midpoint of the eyebrow arch, and the lower lower edge of the orbit is sunken.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
