所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：1. 哮喘，支气管炎，支气管哮喘，百日咳。 2. 落枕，肩背痛。 (1. Asthma, bronchitis, bronchial asthma, whooping cough.2. Stiff pillow, shoulder and back pain.)
位置：大椎旁开0.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (0.5 inches apart from the big vertebra.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
