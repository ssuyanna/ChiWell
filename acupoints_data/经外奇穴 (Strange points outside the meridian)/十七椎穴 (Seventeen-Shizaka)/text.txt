所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：腰骶痛，腰腿痛，下肢瘫痪，崩漏，痛经，月经不调，遗尿，转胞，胎位不正。 (Lumbar sacral pain, lumbar and leg pain, lower limb paralysis, melanoma, dysmenorrhea, irregular menstruation, enuresis, cellular reversal, and irregular fetal position.)
位置：在腰部，当后正中线上，第5腰椎棘突下。 (On the waist, on the posterior median line, under the spinous process of the 5th lumbar vertebra.)
