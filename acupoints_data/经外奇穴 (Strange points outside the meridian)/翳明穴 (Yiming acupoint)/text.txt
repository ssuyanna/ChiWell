所属经脉：经外奇穴 (Strange points outside the meridian)
主调病症：头痛，眩晕，目疾，耳鸣，失眠。 (Headache, dizziness, eye disease, tinnitus, insomnia.)
位置：在翳风穴（翳风与风池连线的中点）后1寸处。 （此穴为双穴，左右各一） (1 inch behind the Yifeng point (the midpoint of the line connecting Yifeng and Fengchi).(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
