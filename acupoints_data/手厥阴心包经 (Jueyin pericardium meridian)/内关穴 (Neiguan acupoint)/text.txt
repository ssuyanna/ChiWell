所属经脉：手厥阴心包经 (Jueyin pericardium meridian)
主调病症：晕车，头痛，眼睛充血，上腹痛，月经痛，腹泻，心痛，惊悸，胃痛，呕吐，呃逆，健忘，失眠，胸胁痛，癫狂，痫症，疟疾，肘臂疼痛，休克，心绞痛，心律不齐，神经衰弱，精神分裂症，癔病，无脉症等。 (Motion sickness, headache, congestion in eyes, upper abdominal pain, menstrual pain, diarrhea, heartache, palpitations, stomachache, vomiting, hiccups, forgetfulness, insomnia, chest and flank pain, epilepsy, malaria, elbow and arm pain, shock, angina pectoris, arrhythmia, neurasthenia, schizophrenia, hysteria, pulselessness, etc.)
位置：在前臂掌侧，当曲泽与大陵的连线上，腕横纹上2寸，掌长肌腱与桡侧腕屈肌腱之间。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the palm of the forearm, on the line between Quze and Daling, 2 inches above the horizontal line of the wrist, between the long tendon of the palm and the flexor wrist tendon on the radial side.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
