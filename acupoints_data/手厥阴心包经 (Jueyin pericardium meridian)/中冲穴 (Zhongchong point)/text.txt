所属经脉：手厥阴心包经 (Jueyin pericardium meridian)
主调病症：中风昏迷，热病心烦，身热如火，小儿夜啼、舌强肿痛，晕厥，头痛，心痛，中暑，惊风等。 (Stroke coma, fever, body heat like fire, children cry at night, strong swelling and painful tongue, fainting, headache, heartache, heat stroke, convulsion, etc.)
位置：在手中指末节尖端中央。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the center of the tip of the end of the middle finger.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
