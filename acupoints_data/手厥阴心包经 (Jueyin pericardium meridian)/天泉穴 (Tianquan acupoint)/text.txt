所属经脉：手厥阴心包经 (Jueyin pericardium meridian)
主调病症：1. 循环系统疾病：心绞痛，心动过速，心内膜炎； 2. 精神神经系统疾病：肋间神经痛，膈肌痉挛； 3. 其它：臂痛，支气管炎，咳嗽，视力减退等。 (1. Circulatory system diseases: angina pectoris, tachycardia, endocarditis; 2. Psychiatric neurological diseases: intercostal neuralgia, diaphragm spasm; 3. Others: arm pain, bronchitis, cough, vision loss, etc.)
位置：在臂内侧，当腋前纹头下2寸，肱二头肌的长、短头之间；或于曲泽穴上7寸取穴。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the inside of the arm, 2 inches below the head of the axillary, between the long and short heads of the biceps; or 7 inches above the Quze point.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
