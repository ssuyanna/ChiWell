所属经脉：手厥阴心包经 (Jueyin pericardium meridian)
主调病症：1. 循环系统疾病：心痛，风湿性心脏病，心绞痛，心肌炎，心脏内外膜炎； 2. 精神神经系统疾病：癫痫，癔病，精神分裂症，脑血管病后遗症； 3. 其它：感冒，咽喉炎，胃炎，胃痛，呕吐，热病烦躁，胸痛，疟疾，癫狂，痫症，肘挛，臂痛，荨麻疹，子宫内膜炎等。 (1. Circulatory system diseases: heartache, rheumatic heart disease, angina pectoris, myocarditis, internal and external mesticitis of the heart; 2. Psychiatric neurological diseases: epilepsy, hysteria, schizophrenia, sequelae of cerebrovascular disease; 3. Others: colds, throat inflammation, gastritis, stomach pain, vomiting, fever irritability, chest pain, malaria, epilepsy, elbow cramps, arm pain, urticaria, endometriitis, etc.)
位置：在前臂掌侧，当曲泽与大陵的连线上，腕横纹上3寸，掌长肌腱与桡侧腕屈肌腱之间。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the palm of the forearm, on the line between Quze and Daling, 3 inches above the horizontal line of the wrist, between the long tendon of the palm and the flexor wrist tendon on the radial side.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
