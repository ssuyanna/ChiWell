所属经脉：手厥阴心包经 (Jueyin pericardium meridian)
主调病症：心痛，胸痛，心悸，热病烦躁，胃痛，呕吐，中暑，泄泻，肘挛，臂痛，瘾诊等。 (Heartache, chest pain, palpitations, irritability in fever, stomach pain, vomiting, heat stroke, diarrhea, elbow cramps, arm pain, addiction diagnosis, etc.)
位置：在肘横纹中，当肱二头肌腱的尺侧缘。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the transverse elbow, when the ulnar edge of the biceps tendon.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
