所属经脉：手厥阴心包经 (Jueyin pericardium meridian)
主调病症：心痛，心悸，呕血，衄血，胸胁痛，肘臂痛，腋肿，疔疮，癫痫。 (Heartache, palpitations, vomiting of blood, epistaxis, chest and flank pain, elbow and arm pain, arm swelling, boils, epilepsy.)
位置：在前臂掌侧，当曲泽与大陵的连线上，腕横纹上5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the palm of the forearm, on the connection between Quze and Daling, the horizontal line of the wrist is 5 inches above.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
