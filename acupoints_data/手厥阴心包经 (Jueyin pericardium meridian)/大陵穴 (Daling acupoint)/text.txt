所属经脉：手厥阴心包经 (Jueyin pericardium meridian)
主调病症：心痛，惊悸，胃痛，呕逆，吐血，癫狂，痫症，腕关节痛，胸胁痛等。 (Heartache, palpitations, stomach pain, vomiting, vomiting, epilepsy, wrist pain, chest and flank pain, etc.)
位置：在腕掌横纹的中点处，当掌长肌腱与桡侧腕屈肌腱之间。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (At the midpoint of the transverse line of the wrist, between the long tendon of the palm and the flexor of the radial wrist.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
