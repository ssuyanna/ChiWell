所属经脉：手厥阴心包经 (Jueyin pericardium meridian)
主调病症：胸闷，咳嗽，气喘，胁肋疼痛，乳痈，瘰疬，腋下肿痛等。 (Chest tightness, cough, asthma, pain in the ribs, breast abscess, scrotum, swelling and pain under the armpits, etc.)
位置：在胸部，当第四肋间隙，乳头外1寸，前正中线旁开5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the chest, when the fourth intercostal space is 1 inch outside the nipple, 5 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
