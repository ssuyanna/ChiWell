所属经脉：手厥阴心包经 (Jueyin pericardium meridian)
主调病症：心痛，心悸，癫狂，癫痫，口疮，口臭，中风，善怒，发热无汗，两便带血，胸胁支满，黄疸，昏迷，晕厥，中暑，呕吐，鹅掌风等。 (Heartache, palpitations, madness, epilepsy, mouth sores, bad breath, stroke, good anger, fever and no sweating, bloody stools, chest and flank branches, jaundice, coma, fainting, heat stroke, vomiting, goose palm wind, etc.)
位置：在手掌心，当第2、3掌骨之间偏于第3掌骨，握拳屈指时中指尖处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the palm of your hand, when the second and third metacarpal bones are biased to the third metacarpal bones, clench your fists and bend your fingers.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
