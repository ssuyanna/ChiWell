所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：1. 运动系统疾病：肩周炎，膝关节炎，下肢瘫痪，两足无力，转筋，跗肿，肩臂痛； 2. 其它：胃痉挛，肠炎，扁桃体炎，脘腹疼痛，脚气等。 (1. Motor system diseases: periarthritis of the shoulder, knee arthritis, paralysis of the lower limbs, weakness of both feet, tendons, swelling of the tars, shoulder and arm pain; 2. Others: stomach cramps, enteritis, tonsillitis, abdominal pain, athlete's foot, etc.)
位置：在小腿前外侧，当犊鼻下8寸，距胫骨前缘一横指。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the anterior and lateral side of the calf, 8 inches below the calf’s nose, one horizontal finger from the front edge of the tibia.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
