所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：咽喉肿痛，咯血，气喘，瘰疬，瘿气，高血压。 (Swelling and sore throat, hemoptysis, asthma, scrofula, goiter, high blood pressure.)
位置：在颈部，结喉旁开1.5寸，胸锁乳突肌的前缘，颈总动脉搏动处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the neck, 1.5 inches apart from the throat, the anterior edge of the sternocleidomastoid muscle, and the common carotid artery pulsation.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
