所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：目痛多泪，眼睑瞤动不止，迎风泪出，目视物不明，结膜炎，视力减退，偏头痛，前额神经痛，血管性头痛，精神分裂症，面神经麻痹，中风后遗症，高血压病等。 (Eye pain and tears, eyelids are constantly moving, tears come out in the wind, visual objects are unknown, conjunctivitis, vision loss, migraine, forehead neuralgia, vascular headache, schizophrenia, facial nerve paralysis, sequelae of stroke, hypertension, etc.)
位置：在头侧部，当额角发际上0.5寸，头正中线旁开4.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the side of the head, 0.5 inches on the forehead and 4.5 inches away from the midline of the head.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
