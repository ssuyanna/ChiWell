所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：胃痛，吐血，呕吐，食欲不振，腹胀，纳呆，吞酸，肠鸣，泄泻等。 (Stomach pain, vomiting blood, vomiting, loss of appetite, abdominal distension, impotence, soreness, intestinal rumbling, diarrhea, etc.)
位置：在上腹部，当脐中上5寸。距前正中线2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the upper abdomen, 5 inches above the navel.2 inches from the front center line.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
