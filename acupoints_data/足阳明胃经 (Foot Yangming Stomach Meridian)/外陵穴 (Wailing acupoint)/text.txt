所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：腹痛，泄泻，痢疾，痛经，疝气，腹胀等。 (Abdominal pain, diarrhea, dysentery, dysmenorrhea, hernia, abdominal distension, etc.)
位置：在下腹部，当脐中下1寸，距前正中线2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the lower abdomen, 1 inch below the navel, 2 inches from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
