所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：小腹胀痛，小便不利，遗尿，便秘，月经不调，疝气等。 (Bloating and pain in the lower abdomen, difficulty in urination, enuresis, constipation, irregular menstruation, hernia, etc.)
位置：在下腹部，当脐中下3寸，距前正中线2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the lower abdomen, 3 inches below the navel, 2 inches from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
