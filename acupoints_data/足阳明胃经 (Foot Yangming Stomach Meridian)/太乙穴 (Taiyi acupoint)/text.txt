所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：胃疼，腹胀，心烦，癫狂等。 (Stomach pain, abdominal distension, upset, madness, etc.)
位置：在上腹部，当脐中上2寸，距前正中线2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the upper abdomen, 2 inches above the middle of the navel, 2 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
