所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：头痛眩晕，咳嗽多痰，气喘，胸痛，癫狂，痫症，下肢浮肿，腿膝酸痛，下肢痿痹，高血压等。 (Headache and dizziness, cough and phlegm, asthma, chest pain, madness, epilepsy, swelling of lower limbs, sore legs and knees, lower limb paralysis, hypertension, etc.)
位置：外踝尖上8寸，条口穴外1寸，胫骨前嵴外2横指处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (8 inches above the tip of the lateral ankle, 1 inch outside the tip of the tibia, and 2 horizontal fingers outside the anterior ridge of the tibia.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
