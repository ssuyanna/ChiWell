所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：发热，头痛，牙痛，鼻衄，咽喉肿痛，腹痛，腹胀，泄泻，痢疾，便秘，便血，瘾疹，足背肿痛，跖趾关节痛等。 (Fever, headache, toothache, epistaxis, sore throat, abdominal pain, abdominal distension, diarrhea, dysentery, constipation, bloody stool, rash, swollen and painful feet, metatarsophalal joint pain, etc.)
位置：在足背，当2、3趾间，趾蹼缘后方赤白肉际处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back of the foot, between the 2 and 3 toes, at the red and white flesh at the back of the webbing of the toes.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
