所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：口眼歪斜，流涎，眼睑瞤动，齿痛，颊肿，面神经麻痹，三叉神经痛等。 (Crooked mouth and eyes, salivation, eyelid twitching, toothache, swelling of cheeks, facial nerve paralysis, trigeminal neuralgia, etc.)
位置：在面部，口角外侧，上直瞳孔。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the face, outside the corner of the mouth, the upper straight pupil.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
