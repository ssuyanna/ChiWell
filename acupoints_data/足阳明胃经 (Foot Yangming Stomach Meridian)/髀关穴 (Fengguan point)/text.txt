所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：膝、髋、股痛，下肢屈伸不利、麻痹、瘫痪，腿膝肿痛，股外侧皮神经炎，腹股沟淋巴结炎等。 (Knee, hip, and femoral pain, poor flexion and extension of the lower limbs, paralysis, swollen and painful legs and knees, lateral femoral dermatitis, inguinal lymphadenitis, etc.)
位置：在大腿前面，当髂前上棘与髌底外侧端的连线上，屈股时，平会阴，居缝匠肌外侧凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In front of the thigh, when the connection between the anterior superior iliac spine and the basal lateral end of the patellar is flexed, it is flat on the perineum and is located in the depression on the lateral side of the sartois muscle.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
