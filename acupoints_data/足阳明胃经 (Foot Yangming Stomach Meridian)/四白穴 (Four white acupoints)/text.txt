所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：1. 精神神经系统疾病：三叉神经痛，面神经麻痹，面肌痉挛； 2. 五官科系统疾病：角膜炎，近视，青光眼，夜盲，结膜瘙痒，角膜白斑，鼻窦炎； 3. 其它：胆道蛔虫症，头痛，眩晕。 (1. Psychiatric neurological diseases: trigeminal neuralgia, facial nerve paralysis, facial muscle spasm; 2. Oentreological system diseases: keratitis, myopia, glaucoma, night blindness, conjunctival itching, corneal leukoplakia, sinusitis; 3. Others: biliary ascariasis, headache, dizziness.)
位置：位于下眼睑下一寸，当眶下孔处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located one inch below the lower eyelid, at the infraorbital foramen.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
