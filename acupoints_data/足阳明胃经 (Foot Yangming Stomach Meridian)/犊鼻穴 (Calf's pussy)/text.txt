所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：膝痛，下肢麻痹，屈伸不利，脚气。 (Knee pain, lower limb paralysis, poor flexion and extension, and athlete's foot.)
位置：在膝部，髌骨与髌韧带外侧凹陷中。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the knee, the patella and the lateral depression of the patellar ligament.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
