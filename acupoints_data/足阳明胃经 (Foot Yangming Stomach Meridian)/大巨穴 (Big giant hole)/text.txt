所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：腹痛，腹泻，小腹胀满，小便不利，疝气，遗精，阳痿，早泄，腹直肌痉挛，肠梗阻，膀胱炎，尿潴留等。 (Abdominal pain, diarrhea, bloating in the lower abdomen, difficulty in urination, hernia, nocturnal emission, premature ejaculation, rectus abdomen spasm, intestinal obstruction, cystitis, urinary retention, etc.)
位置：在下腹部，当脐中下2寸，距前正中线2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the lower abdomen, 2 inches below the navel, 2 inches from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
