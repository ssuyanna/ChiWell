所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：胃痛，呕吐，纳呆，泄泻，便溏，消化性溃疡病，急、慢性胃炎，胃下垂等。 (Stomach pain, vomiting, swelling, diarrhea, loose stools, peptic ulcer disease, acute and chronic gastritis, gastric sagging, etc.)
位置：在上腹部，当脐中上4寸，距前正中线2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the upper abdomen, 4 inches above the middle of the navel, 2 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
