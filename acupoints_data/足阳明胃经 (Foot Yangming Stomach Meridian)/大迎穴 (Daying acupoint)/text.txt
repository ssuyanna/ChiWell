所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：齿痛，颊肿，牙关紧闭，口歪，及面神经麻痹，腮腺炎等。 (Toothache, swelling of cheeks, tight teeth, crooked mouth, facial nerve paralysis, mumps, etc.)
位置：在面部，下颌角前方咬肌附着部前缘，当面动脉搏动处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the face, the front edge of the masseter muscle attachment at the front of the mandibular angle, where the artery pulsates in the face.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
