所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：胃痉挛，腹泻，膝盖痛、浮肿，膝胫痹痛，鹤膝风，胃痛，乳痈等。 (Stomach cramps, diarrhea, knee pain, swelling, knee tibia pain, knee wind, stomach pain, breast abscess, etc.)
位置：在大腿前面，当髂前上棘与髌底外侧端的连线上，髌底上2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In front of the thigh, 2 inches above the patellar base when the line between the anterior superior iliac spine and the lateral end of the basal base.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
