所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：腹痛，疝气，月经不调，闭经，崩漏，带下，阴挺，遗精，阳痿等。 (Abdominal pain, hernia, irregular menstruation, amenorrhea, melancholy, hypoplasia, yin protrusion, nocturnal emission, impotence, etc.)
位置：在下腹部，当脐中下4寸，距前正中线2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the lower abdomen, 4 inches below the navel, 2 inches from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
