所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：本穴只作胸腹部腧穴的定位标志——两乳头之间作8寸。 (This acupoint is only used as a positioning mark for the acupoints in the chest and abdomen - 8 inches between the two nipples.)
位置：在胸部，当第4肋间隙，乳头中央，距前正中线4寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the chest, when the 4th intercostal space, the center of the nipple, 4 inches from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
