所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：1. 五官科系统疾病：急慢性结膜炎，近视，远视，散光，青光眼，色盲，夜盲症，睑缘炎，角膜炎，视神经炎，视神经萎缩，白内障，视网膜色素变性，口眼歪斜，眶下神经痛； 2. 精神神经系统疾病：面肌痉挛，面神经麻痹。 (1. Oentangial system diseases: acute and chronic conjunctivitis, myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism, glaucoma, color blindness, nocturnal blindness, blepharitis, keratitis, optic neuritis, optic atrophy, cataracts, retinal pigment degeneration, oral and eye crook, infraorbital neuralgia; 2. Psychiatric neurological diseases: facial spasm, facial nerve paralysis.)
位置：在面部，瞳孔直下，当眼球与眶下缘之间。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the face, the pupil is straight down, between the eyeball and the infraorbital edge.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
