所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽，气喘，缺盆中痛，胸部满闷，喉痹，瘰疬，瘿瘤等。 (Cough, asthma, pelvic pain, chest tightness, throat numbness, scrotum, goiter, etc.)
位置：位于锁骨上窝中央，胸正中线旁开4寸处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located in the center of the supraclavicular fossa, 4 inches away from the midline of the chest.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
