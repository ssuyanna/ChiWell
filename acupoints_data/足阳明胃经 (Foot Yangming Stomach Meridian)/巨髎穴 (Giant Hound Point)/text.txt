所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：口眼歪斜，眼睑瞤动，鼻塞，鼻衄，齿痛，唇颊肿，三叉神经痛等。 (Crooked mouth and eyes, eyelids, nasal congestion, epistaxis, toothache, swelling of lips and cheeks, trigeminal neuralgia, etc.)
位置：在面部，瞳孔直下，平鼻翼下缘处，当鼻唇沟外侧。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the face, the pupil is straight down, at the lower edge of the flat nose wing, and on the lateral side of the nasolabial groove.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
