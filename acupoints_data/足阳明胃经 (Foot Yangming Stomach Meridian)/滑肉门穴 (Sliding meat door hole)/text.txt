所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：胃痛，呕吐，呃逆，肠鸣，泄泻，癫狂，减肥塑身等。 (Stomach pain, vomiting, hiccups, intestinal rumbling, diarrhea, madness, weight loss and body shaping, etc.)
位置：在上腹部，当脐中上1寸，距前正中线2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the upper abdomen, 1 inch above the middle of the navel, 2 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
