所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，面肿，齿痛，癫狂痫，胃病，口眼歪斜，胃脘痛，足背肿痛，足缓不收等。 (Headache, swelling of the face, toothache, epilepsy, stomach problems, crooked mouth and eyes, epigastric pain, swelling and painful feet, slow feet and not retracting, etc.)
位置：在足背最高处，当拇长伸肌腱与趾长伸肌腱之间，足背动脉搏动处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (At the highest point of the dorsum of the foot, between the extensor longus tendon and the extensor longus tendon, the dorsal artery pulsation of the dorsal foot.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
