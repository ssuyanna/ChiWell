所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：1. 五官科系统疾病：牙痛，牙髓炎，冠周炎，腮腺炎，下颌关节炎，咬肌痉挛； 2. 精神神经系统疾病：面神经麻痹，三叉神经痛； 3. 其它：脑血管病后遗症，甲状腺肿。 (1. Oentreological system diseases: toothache, pulpitis, pericoric coronary inflammation, mumps, mandibular arthritis, masseter muscle spasm; 2. Psychiatric neurological diseases: facial nerve paralysis, trigeminal neuralgia; 3. Others: sequelae of cerebrovascular disease, goiter.)
位置：在面颊部，下颌角前上方，耳下大约一横指处，咀嚼时肌肉隆起时出现的凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the cheek, above the front and upper jaw angle, about one horizontal finger under the ear, the depression that appears when the muscles bulge when chewing.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
