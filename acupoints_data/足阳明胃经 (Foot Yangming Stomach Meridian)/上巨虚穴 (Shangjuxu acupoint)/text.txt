所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：1. 消化系统疾病：阑尾炎，胃肠炎，泄泻，痢疾，疝气，便秘，消化不良，肠痈； 2. 运动系统疾病：脑血管病后遗症，下肢麻痹或痉挛，膝关节肿痛； 3. 其他：脚气等。 (1. Digestive system diseases: appendicitis, gastroenteritis, diarrhea, dysentery, hernia, constipation, indigestion, intestinal abscess; 2. Motor system diseases: sequelae of cerebrovascular disease, lower limb paralysis or spasm, swelling and pain in the knee joint; 3. Others: athlete's foot, etc.)
位置：在小腿前外侧，当犊鼻下6寸，距胫骨前缘一横指；或于足三里与下巨虚连线的中点取穴。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the anterior and outer side of the calf, 6 inches below the calf’s nose, one horizontal finger from the front edge of the tibia; or select the acupoint at the midpoint of the line connecting Zusanli and Xiajiu.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
