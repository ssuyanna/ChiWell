所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽、喘息、咽喉肿痛等。 (Cough, wheezing, sore throat, etc.)
位置：在颈部，胸锁乳突肌的前缘，当人迎与气舍连线的中点。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the neck, the front edge of the sternocleidomastoid muscle is the midpoint of the connection between the Renying and Qishe.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
