所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，眩晕，目赤，腹胀，便秘，癫狂，头面浮肿，下肢痿痹，脚腕无力，踝关节疾患等。 (Headache, dizziness, red eyes, abdominal distension, constipation, madness, swelling of the head and face, numbness of the lower limbs, weakness of the ankle, ankle joint diseases, etc.)
位置：足背踝关节横纹中央凹陷处，当拇长伸肌踺与趾长伸肌腱之间。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (The central depression of the transverse lines of the dorsal ankle joint, between the extensor longus maxillus longus tendons.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
