所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：胃痛，腹胀，呕吐，纳呆，噫酸，以及急、慢性胃炎，胃下垂，消化性溃疡，胆绞痛等。 (Stomach pain, abdominal distension, vomiting, swelling, belching, as well as acute and chronic gastritis, gastric sagging, peptic ulcers, bile colic, etc.)
位置：在上腹部，当脐中上6寸，距前正中线2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the upper abdomen, 6 inches above the middle of the navel, 2 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
