所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：膝腿麻痹，膝冷，酸痛，屈伸不利，下肢不遂，腰痛，寒疝，腹胀腹痛，脚气，股外侧皮神经炎等。 (Knee and leg paralysis, knee cold, soreness, difficulty in flexion and extension, lower limb absion, low back pain, cold hernia, abdominal distension and abdominal pain, athlete's foot, lateral femoral dermatitis, etc.)
位置：在大腿前面，当髂前上棘与髌底外侧端的连线上，髌底上6寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In front of the thigh, 6 inches above the patellar base when the line between the anterior superior iliac spine and the lateral end of the basal base.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
