所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽，气喘，胸胁胀痛，咳吐脓血等。 (Cough, asthma, chest and flank swelling, coughing, pus and blood, etc.)
位置：在胸部，当第1肋间隙，距前正中线4寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the chest, when the first intercostal space is 4 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
