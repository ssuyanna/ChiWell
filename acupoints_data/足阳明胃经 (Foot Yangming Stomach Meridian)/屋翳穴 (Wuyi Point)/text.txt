所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽，气喘，咳吐脓血，胸胁胀痛，乳痈等。 (Cough, asthma, coughing of pus and blood, chest and flank swelling and pain, breast abscess, etc.)
位置：在胸部，当第2肋间隙，距前正中线4寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the chest, when the second intercostal space is 4 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
