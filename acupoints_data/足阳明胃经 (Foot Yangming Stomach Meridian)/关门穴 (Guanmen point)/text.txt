所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：腹痛，腹胀，肠鸣，泄泻，纳呆，身肿等。 (Abdominal pain, abdominal distension, intestinal rumbling, diarrhea, impotence, body swelling, etc.)
位置：在上腹部，当脐中上3寸，距前正中线2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the upper abdomen, 3 inches above the middle of the navel, 2 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
