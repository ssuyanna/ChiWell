所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽，气喘，胸胁胀满，吐血，呃逆，胸痛等。 (Cough, asthma, chest and flanks, vomiting blood, hiccups, chest pain, etc.)
位置：在胸部，当锁骨中点下缘，距前正中线4寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the chest, at the lower edge of the midpoint of the clavicle, 4 inches from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
