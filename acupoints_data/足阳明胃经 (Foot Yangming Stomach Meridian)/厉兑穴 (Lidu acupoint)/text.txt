所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：鼻衄，牙痛，面肿，咽喉肿痛，腹胀，胃脘疼痛，热病，多梦，癫狂。 (Epiphany, toothache, swelling of the face, sore throat, abdominal distension, gastric pain, fever, dreaming, and madness.)
位置：在足第2趾末节外侧，距趾甲角0.1寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the outside of the end of the second toe of the foot, 0.1 inch from the toenail angle.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
