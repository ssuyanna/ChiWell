所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：腹痛胀满，肠鸣泄痢，面目浮肿，目赤痛，疝气，足背肿痛等。 (Abdominal pain and fullness, intestinal rumbling and diarrhea, swelling of the face, red eyes, hernia, swelling and pain on the back of the feet, etc.)
位置：在足背，当第2、3跖骨结合部前方凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the dorsum of the foot, when the depression in front of the junction of the second and third metatarsal bones.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
