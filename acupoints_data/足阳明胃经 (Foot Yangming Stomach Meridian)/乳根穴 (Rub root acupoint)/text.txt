所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：1. 妇产科系统疾病：乳汁不足，乳痈，乳腺炎； 2. 呼吸系统疾病：咳嗽，哮喘，慢性支气管炎，胸痛，胸膜炎； 3. 精神神经系统疾病：肋间神经痛，臂丛神经痛。 (1. Obstetric and gynecological system diseases: milk deficiency, mastitis, mastitis; 2. Respiratory system diseases: cough, asthma, chronic bronchitis, chest pain, pleurisitis; 3. Psychiatric nervous system diseases: intercostal neuralgia, brachial plexus neuralgia.)
位置：在胸部，当乳头直下，乳房根部，第5肋间隙，距前正中线4寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the chest, when the nipple is straight down, the base of the breast, the 5th intercostal space, 4 inches from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
