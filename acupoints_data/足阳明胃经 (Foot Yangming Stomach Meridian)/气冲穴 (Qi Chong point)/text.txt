所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：疝气，偏坠，睾丸肿痛，小便淋漓，月经不调，带下，难产，遗精，阳痿，肠鸣腹痛，月经不调，不孕，阴肿等。 (Hernia, sideways, swelling and painful testicles, dripping urine, irregular menstruation, dysfunction of delivery, nocturnal emission, impotence, intestinal rumbling and abdominal pain, irregular menstruation, infertility, swelling of the vagina, etc.)
位置：在腹股沟稍上方，当脐中下5寸，距前正中线2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (A little above the groin, 5 inches below the navel, 2 inches from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
