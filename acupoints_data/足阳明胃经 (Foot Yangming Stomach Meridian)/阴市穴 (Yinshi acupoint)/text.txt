所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：膝关节及周围软组织疾患，腿膝麻痹，酸痛，伸屈不利，下肢肿胀，瘫痪不遂，脚气，腰痛，寒疝，腹胀，腹痛。 (Diseases of knee joints and surrounding soft tissues, leg and knee paralysis, soreness, difficulty in stretching and flexion, swelling of the lower limbs, paralysis, athlete's foot, low back pain, cold hernia, abdominal distension, and abdominal pain.)
位置：在大腿前面，当髂前上棘与髌骨外缘的连线上，髌底上3寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In front of the thigh, 3 inches above the base of the patellar when the line between the anterior superior iliac spine and the outer edge of the patellar.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
