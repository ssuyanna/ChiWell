所属经脉：足阳明胃经 (Foot Yangming Stomach Meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽，气喘，胸肋胀痛，乳痈。 (Cough, asthma, chest and rib swelling, breast abscess.)
位置：在胸部，当第3肋间隙，距前正中线4寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the chest, when the third intercostal space is 4 inches from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
