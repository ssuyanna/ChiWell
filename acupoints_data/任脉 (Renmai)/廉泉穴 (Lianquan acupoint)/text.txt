所属经脉：任脉 (Renmai)
主调病症：舌强，舌下神经麻痹，流涎，语言不清，哑证，失语，口腔炎，口腔溃疡，急性咽炎。 (Strong tongue, sublingual nerve paralysis, salivation, unclear speech, dumb symptoms, aphasia, stomatitis, oral ulcers, acute pharyngitis.)
位置：位于颈上部正中，于下颌下缘与舌骨体之间，下颌下缘1寸的凹陷处取穴。 (Located in the middle of the upper part of the neck, select the acupoint between the lower edge of the mandibular and the hyoid body, and the depression at 1 inch lower edge of the mandibular.)
