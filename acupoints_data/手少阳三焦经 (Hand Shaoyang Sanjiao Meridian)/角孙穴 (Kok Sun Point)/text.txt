所属经脉：手少阳三焦经 (Hand Shaoyang Sanjiao Meridian)
主调病症：耳部红肿，目赤肿痛，目翳，颊肿，齿痛，唇燥，项强，头痛。 (Red and swollen ears, red and swollen eyes, cataracts, swelling cheeks, toothache, dry lips, strong neck, and headache.)
位置：在头部，折耳廓向前，当耳尖直上入发际处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the head, fold the auricle forward, and go straight to the hairline with the tip of the ear straight up.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
