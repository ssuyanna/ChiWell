所属经脉：手少阳三焦经 (Hand Shaoyang Sanjiao Meridian)
主调病症：1. 头面五官科疾病：耳聋耳鸣，口噤不开，齿痛，颊肿，头痛，腮腺炎，下颌关节炎，口眼歪斜，笑肌麻痹，甲状腺肿； 2. 精神神经系统疾病：痉病，狂疾，膈肌痉挛，面神经麻痹。 (1. Head and facial ENT diseases: deafness, tinnitus, inability to open the mouth, toothache, swelling of cheeks, headache, mumps, mandibular arthritis, crooked mouth and eyes, paralysis of the laughing muscles, goiter; 2. Mental and neurological diseases: spasm, crazy disease, diaphragm spasm, facial nerve paralysis.)
位置：在耳垂后方，当乳突与下颌角之间的凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Behind the earlobe, the depression between the mastoid and the mandibular angle.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
