所属经脉：手少阳三焦经 (Hand Shaoyang Sanjiao Meridian)
主调病症：肩臂痛，瘿气，瘰疬，上肢麻痹等。 (Shoulder and arm pain, goiter, scrofula, upper limb paralysis, etc.)
位置：在臂外侧，当肘尖与肩髎的连线上，肩髎下3寸，三角肌的后下缘。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the outside of the arm, on the line between the tip of the elbow and the shoulder, 3 inches below the shoulder, and the posterior lower edge of the deltoid muscle.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
