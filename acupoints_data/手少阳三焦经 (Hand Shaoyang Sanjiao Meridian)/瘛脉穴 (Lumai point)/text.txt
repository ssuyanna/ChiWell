所属经脉：手少阳三焦经 (Hand Shaoyang Sanjiao Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，耳聋，耳鸣，小儿惊痫，呕吐，泄痢。 (Headache, deafness, tinnitus, epilepsy in children, vomiting, and diarrhea.)
位置：在头部，耳后乳突中央，当角孙与翳风之间，沿耳轮连线的中、下1/3的交点处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the head, in the center of the mastoid process behind the ear, at the intersection between the horns and the Yifeng, along the middle and lower 1/3 of the line connecting the earlobe.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
