所属经脉：手少阳三焦经 (Hand Shaoyang Sanjiao Meridian)
主调病症：1. 头面五官疾病：耳聋耳鸣； 2. 精神神经系统疾病：癫痫； 3. 其他疾病：气滞喘满，臂痛，上肢肌肤痛。 (1. Head, face, and five senses: deafness, tinnitus; 2. Mental and neurological diseases: epilepsy; 3. Other diseases: stagnation of qi, asthma, arm pain, and upper limb skin pain.)
位置：在前臂背侧，当腕背横纹上3寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back of the forearm, 3 inches above the horizontal line on the back of the wrist.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
