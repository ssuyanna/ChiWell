所属经脉：手少阳三焦经 (Hand Shaoyang Sanjiao Meridian)
主调病症：1.五官科疾病：耳聋牙痛，咽喉痛； 2.其他疾病：偏头痛、上肢麻痹瘫痪、神经衰弱、眩晕、肾炎等。 (1. Oentoptic diseases: deafness, toothache, sore throat; 2. Other diseases: migraine, paralysis of upper limbs, neurasthenia, dizziness, nephritis, etc.)
位置：在前臂背侧，肘尖下方5寸，当阳池与肘尖的连线上，尺骨与桡骨之间。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the dorsal side of the forearm, 5 inches below the elbow tip, on the line between the yang pond and the elbow tip, between the ulna and the radius.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
