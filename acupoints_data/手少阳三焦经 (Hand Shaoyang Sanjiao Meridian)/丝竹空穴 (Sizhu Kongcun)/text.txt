所属经脉：手少阳三焦经 (Hand Shaoyang Sanjiao Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，目眩，目赤痛，目翳，眼睑瞤动，视物不明，癫痫，恶风寒等。 (Headache, dizziness, redness and pain in the eyes, cataracts of the eyes, eyelid movements, unclear vision, epilepsy, aversion to wind and cold, etc.)
位置：在面部，当眉梢凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the face, when the eyebrow tips are sunken.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
