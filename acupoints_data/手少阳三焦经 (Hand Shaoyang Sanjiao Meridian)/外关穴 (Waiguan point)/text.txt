所属经脉：手少阳三焦经 (Hand Shaoyang Sanjiao Meridian)
主调病症：感冒，头痛，发热，耳鸣，耳聋，目痛，咽肿，口眼歪斜，瘰疬，胸胁痛，手颤指麻，肘臂屈伸不利，上肢痹痛，落枕，急性腰扭伤等。 (Cold, headache, fever, tinnitus, deafness, eye pain, swollen throat, crooked mouth and eyes, scrotum, chest and flank pain, trembling hands and fingers, poor flexion and extension of elbows and arms, paralysis of upper limbs, stiff neck, acute lumbar sprain, etc.)
位置：在前臂背侧，当阳池与肘尖的连线上，腕背横纹上2寸，尺骨与桡骨之间。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the dorsal side of the forearm, on the line between the yang pond and the tip of the elbow, 2 inches above the horizontal line of the wrist dorsal, between the ulna and the radius.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
