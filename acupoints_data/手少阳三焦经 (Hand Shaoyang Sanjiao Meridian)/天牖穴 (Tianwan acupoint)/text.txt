所属经脉：手少阳三焦经 (Hand Shaoyang Sanjiao Meridian)
主调病症：1. 头面五官疾病：头痛头晕，目痛面肿，暴聋耳鸣，视神经炎，鼻衄喉痹； 2. 其他疾病：颈肩背部痉挛强直，瘰疬多梦。 (1. Head, face and five sense diseases: headache, dizziness, eye pain, swelling, violent deafness, tinnitus, optic neuritis, epistaxis and throat numbness; 2. Other diseases: cramps, stiff neck, shoulders, back, and back, and scrotum and dreams.)
位置：在颈侧部，当乳突的后方直下，平下颌角，胸锁乳突肌的后缘；或于天容穴与天柱穴连线的中点处取穴。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the side of the neck, when the posterior part of the mastoid process is straight down, the mandibular angle is flat, and the posterior edge of the sternocleidomastoid muscle; or select the acupoint at the midpoint of the line connecting Tianrong point and Tianzhu point.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
