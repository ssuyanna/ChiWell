所属经脉：手少阳三焦经 (Hand Shaoyang Sanjiao Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，发热，目赤，耳痛，耳鸣，耳聋，喉痹，疟疾，手臂痛。 (Headache, fever, red eyes, ear pain, tinnitus, deafness, throat numbness, malaria, and arm pain.)
位置：在手背部，当第4、五指间，指蹼缘后方赤白肉际处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back of the hand, between the 4th and 5th fingers, the red and white flesh at the back of the webbing.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
