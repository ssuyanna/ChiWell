所属经脉：手少阳三焦经 (Hand Shaoyang Sanjiao Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，齿痛、项强、癫疾、臂痛，上肢麻痹等。 (Headache, toothache, neck strength, epilepsy, arm pain, upper limb paralysis, etc.)
位置：在臂外侧，当清冷渊与臑会连线的中点处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the outside of the arm, at the midpoint where the cold abyss and the cervix connect.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
