所属经脉：手少阳三焦经 (Hand Shaoyang Sanjiao Meridian)
主调病症：1. 头面五官疾病：头痛，耳聋，暴喑卒聋，龋齿牙痛； 2. 运动系统疾病：挫闪腰痛，手臂痛不能上举； 3. 其他疾病：恶寒发热无汗，内伤，脑血管后遗症，眼病，失语。 (1. Head, face and five sense diseases: headache, deafness, sudden deafness, tooth decay; 2. Motor system diseases: back pain, pain in the back of the arms, and pain in the arms; 3. Other diseases: chills, fever, no sweating, internal injuries, sequelae of cerebrovascular, eye diseases, aphasia.)
位置：在前臂背侧，当腕背横纹上。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back of the forearm, on the horizontal lines of the wrist.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
