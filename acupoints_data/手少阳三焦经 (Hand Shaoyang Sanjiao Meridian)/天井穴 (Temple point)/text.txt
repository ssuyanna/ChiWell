所属经脉：手少阳三焦经 (Hand Shaoyang Sanjiao Meridian)
主调病症：五官科疾病：眼睑炎，扁桃腺炎，外眼角红肿，咽喉疼痛； 神经系统疾病：中风，忧郁症，精神分裂症； 呼吸系统疾病：支气管炎，颈淋巴结核； 心血管疾病：心痛，胸痛； 其他疾病：偏头痛，颈项痛，肘关节及上肢软组织损伤，落枕，疮肿，荨麻疹，皮肤搔痒症。 (Oentreological diseases: blepharitis, tonsillitis, redness and swelling of the external corners of the eye, sore throat; neurological diseases: stroke, depression, schizophrenia; respiratory diseases: bronchitis, cervical lymph node tract; cardiovascular diseases: heartache, chest pain; other diseases: migraine, neck pain, soft tissue damage to the elbow joints and upper limbs, stiff neck, sores, urticaria, skin thorns.)
位置：在臂外侧，屈肘时，当肘尖直上1寸凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the outside of the arm, when flexing the elbow, the tip of the elbow is straight up to the depression 1 inch.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
