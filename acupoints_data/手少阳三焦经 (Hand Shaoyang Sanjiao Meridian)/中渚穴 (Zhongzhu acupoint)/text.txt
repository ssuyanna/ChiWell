所属经脉：手少阳三焦经 (Hand Shaoyang Sanjiao Meridian)
主调病症：1. 头面部病症：神经性耳聋，聋哑症，头痛头晕，咽喉肿痛，角膜白斑，喉痹； 2. 运动系统病症：肩背部筋膜炎等劳损性疾病，肋间神经痛，手臂红肿疼痛，肘腕关节炎等； 3. 其他病症：疟疾。 (1. Head and facial symptoms: neurodeafness, deafness and dumbness, headache and dizziness, sore throat, leukoplakia, throat paralysis; 2. Motor system symptoms: strained diseases such as shoulder and back fasciitis, intercostal neuralgia, redness, swollen and pain in the arms, elbow and wrist arthritis, etc.; 3. Other symptoms: malaria.)
位置：在手背部，当环指本节（掌指关节）的后方，第4、5掌骨间凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back of the hand, when the ring finger is behind this section (metachondral joint), the depression between the 4th and 5th metacarpal bones.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
