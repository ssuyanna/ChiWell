所属经脉：手少阳三焦经 (Hand Shaoyang Sanjiao Meridian)
主调病症：肩臂痛，上肢麻痹或瘫痪，肩关节周围炎等。 (Shoulder and arm pain, upper limb paralysis or paralysis, shoulder periarthritis, etc.)
位置：在肩部，肩髃后方，当臂外展时，于肩峰后下方呈现凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the shoulder, behind the shoulder, when the arms are abducted, a depression appears below the back of the acromion.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
