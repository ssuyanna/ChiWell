所属经脉：手少阳三焦经 (Hand Shaoyang Sanjiao Meridian)
主调病症：耳鸣，耳聋，聤耳，齿痛，颌肿，眩晕等。 (Tinnitus, deafness, excessive ears, toothache, swelling jaws, dizziness, etc.)
位置：在面部，当耳屏上切迹的前方，下颌骨髁突后缘，张口有凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the face, when the front of the notch on the tragus is the posterior edge of the mandible condyle, there is a depression in the opening mouth.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
