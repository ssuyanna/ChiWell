所属经脉：手少阳三焦经 (Hand Shaoyang Sanjiao Meridian)
主调病症：腕痛无力，臂肘疼痛，耳聋，疟疾，消渴，头痛，目赤肿痛，喉痹，女性手脚冰凉等。 (Wrist pain, weakness, pain in the arms and elbows, deafness, malaria, diabetes, headache, red and swollen eyes, throat numbness, cold hands and feet of women.)
位置：在腕背横纹中，当指伸肌腱的尺侧缘凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the horizontal lines of the dorsal wrist, it is the depression of the ulnar edge of the finger extensor tendon.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
