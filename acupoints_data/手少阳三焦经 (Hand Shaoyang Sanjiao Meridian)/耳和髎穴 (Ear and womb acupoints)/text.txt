所属经脉：手少阳三焦经 (Hand Shaoyang Sanjiao Meridian)
主调病症：耳鸣，牙关拘急，流涕，口喎，瘈疭，头痛颊肿，面瘫，面肌痉挛，耳炎鼻炎。 (Tinnitus, tooth impotence, runny nose, mouthpatism, cramps, headache, swelling of cheeks, facial paralysis, facial muscle spasm, otitis and rhinitis.)
位置：在头侧部，当鬓发后缘，平耳廓根之前方，颞浅动脉的后缘。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the side of the head, the posterior edge of the temple, the flat auricular root, the posterior edge of the superficial temporal artery.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
