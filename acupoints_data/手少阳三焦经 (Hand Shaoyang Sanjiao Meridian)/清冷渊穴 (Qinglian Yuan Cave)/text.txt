所属经脉：手少阳三焦经 (Hand Shaoyang Sanjiao Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，项强，目黄，肩臂痛等。 (Headache, neck strength, yellow eyes, shoulder and arm pain, etc.)
位置：在臂外侧，屈肘，当肘尖直上2寸，即天井上1寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the outside of the arm, bend the elbow, and 2 inches straight up the tip of the elbow, that is, 1 inch above the patio.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
