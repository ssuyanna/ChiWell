所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：胸满，肋痛，腋下肿，臂痛不举。 (Full chest, rib pain, swollen armpits, and pain in the arms.)
位置：在侧胸部，举臂，当腋中线上，腋下3寸，第4肋间隙中。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the side chest, raise the arm, on the mid-axillary line, 3 inches below the armpit, in the fourth intercostal space.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
