所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：胁肋痛，呕吐，胀满，吞酸，呃逆，黄疸，胃痛，腹胀，以及胆囊炎，肝脏疾患等。 (Flank pain, vomiting, fullness, acid swallowing, hiccups, jaundice, stomach pain, abdominal distension, as well as cholecystitis, liver disease, etc.)
位置：在上腹部，当乳头直下，第七肋间隙，前正中线旁开4寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the upper abdomen, when the nipple is straight down, the seventh intercostal space is 4 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
