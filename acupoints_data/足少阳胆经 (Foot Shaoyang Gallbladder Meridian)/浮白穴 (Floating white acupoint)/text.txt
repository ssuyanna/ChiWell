所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，颈项强痛，耳鸣，耳聋，齿痛，瘰疬，瘿气，臂痛不举，足痿不行。 (Headache, strong neck pain, tinnitus, deafness, toothache, scrofula, goiter, arm pain, and foot impotence.)
位置：在头部，当耳后乳突的后上方，天冲与完骨的弧形连线的中三分与上三分交点。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the head, at the back and upper three points of the arc-shaped connection between Tian Chong and Wan Gu.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
