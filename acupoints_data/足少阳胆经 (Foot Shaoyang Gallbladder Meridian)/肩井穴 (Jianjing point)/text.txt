所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：肩背痹痛，手臂不举，颈项强痛，乳痈，项强，中风偏瘫，滞产，产后血晕，瘰疬，高血压，功能性子宫出血等。 (Shoulder and back pain, arms are not raised, neck pain, breast abscess, neck strength, stroke hemiplegia, stagnation of birth, postpartum blood dizziness, scrofula, hypertension, functional uterine bleeding, etc.)
位置：在肩上，前直乳中，当大椎与肩峰端连线的中点上。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the shoulder, in the front straight breast, at the midpoint of the line connecting the Dazhui and the acromion.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
