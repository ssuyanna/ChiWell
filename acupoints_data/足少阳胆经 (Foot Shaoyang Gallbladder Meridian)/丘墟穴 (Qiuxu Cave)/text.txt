所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：颈项痛，偏头痛，目疾，胆囊炎，胆石症，胸胁胀痛，肋间神经痛，中风偏瘫，下肢痿痹，疟疾，脚气，足跟痛，坐骨神经痛等。 (Neck pain, migraine, eye disease, cholecystitis, cholelithiasis, chest and flank pain, intercostal neuralgia, stroke hemiplegia, lower limb paralysis, malaria, athlete's foot, heel pain, sciatica, etc.)
位置：在足外踝的前下方，当趾长伸肌腱的外侧凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (At the front and bottom of the lateral ankle of the foot, the lateral depression of the extensor tendon of the tympanium.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
