所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，齿龈肿痛，癫痫，惊恐，瘿气。 (Headache, swollen and painful gums, epilepsy, panic, goiter.)
位置：在头部，当耳根后缘直上入发际2寸，率谷后0.5寸处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the head, when the posterior edge of the ear root reaches 2 inches straight up to the hairline, 0.5 inches behind the valley.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
