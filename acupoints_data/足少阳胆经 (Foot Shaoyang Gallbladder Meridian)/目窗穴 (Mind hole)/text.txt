所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，目眩，目赤肿痛，远视，近视，面浮肿，青盲内障，鼻塞，上齿龋肿，小儿惊痫。 (Headache, dizziness, red and swollen eyes, hyperopia, myopia, swelling of the face, blue-blindness, nasal congestion, upper teeth caries, and epilepsy in children.)
位置：在头部，当前发际上1.5寸，头正中线旁开2.25寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the head, the hairline is currently 1.5 inches, and the center line of the head is 2.25 inches away.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
