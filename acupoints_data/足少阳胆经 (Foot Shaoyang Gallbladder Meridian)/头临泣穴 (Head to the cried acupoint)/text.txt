所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，目眩，目赤痛，流泪，目翳，鼻塞，鼻渊，耳聋，小儿惊痫，热病。 (Headache, dizziness, red and painful eyes, tears, eye cataracts, nasal congestion, nasal abyss, deafness, epilepsy in children, and fever.)
位置：在头部，当瞳孔直上入前发际0.5寸，神庭与头维连线的中点处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the head, when the pupil goes straight up to the anterior hairline 0.5 inches, at the midpoint of the connection between the Shenting and the head dimension.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
