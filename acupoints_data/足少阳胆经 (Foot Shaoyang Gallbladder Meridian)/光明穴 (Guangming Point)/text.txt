所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：小腿酸痛，下肢痿痹，偏头痛，目痛，夜盲，近视，癫痫，乳部胀痛，颊肿及白内障等。 (Soreness in the calf, numbness in the lower limbs, migraine, eye pain, night blindness, myopia, epilepsy, breast swelling, cheek swelling and cataracts, etc.)
位置：在小腿外侧，当外踝尖上5寸，腓骨前缘。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the lateral side of the calf, 5 inches above the tip of the lateral ankle, the anterior edge of the fibula.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
