所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，项强，颊肿，齿痛，咽喉肿痛，口眼歪斜，失眠，耳后痛，牙床急痛，项肿不可俯仰，颊肿引耳，喉痹，疟，癫狂痫，偏风，手足挛痿，面瘫，流行性腮腺炎，脑发育不全，脑瘫，癔病。 (Headache, neck strength, swelling of cheeks, toothache, sore throat, crooked mouth and eyes, insomnia, pain behind ears, acute pain in the gums, swelling of neck cannot be raised, swelling of cheeks, ears, throat paralysis, malaria, epilepsy, partial wind, hand and foot cramps, facial paralysis, mumps, brain insufficiency, cerebral palsy, hysteria.)
位置：在头部，当耳后乳突的后下方凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the head, the back and lower depression behind the ear.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
