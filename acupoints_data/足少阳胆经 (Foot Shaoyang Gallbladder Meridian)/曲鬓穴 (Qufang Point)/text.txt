所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：偏头痛，颔颊肿，牙关紧闭，呕吐，齿痛，目赤肿痛，项强不得顾。 (Migraine, swelling chin and cheeks, tight teeth, vomiting, toothache, red, swollen eyes, and strong necks.)
位置：在头部，当耳前鬓角发际后缘的垂线与耳尖水平线交点处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the head, at the intersection of the perpendicular line of the posterior edge of the front sideburns of the ear and the horizontal line of the ear tip.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
