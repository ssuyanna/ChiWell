所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：偏头痛，目外眦痛，胸胁痛，腋下肿痛，瘰疬，脚气，腰腿痛，下肢麻痹，以及坐骨神经痛等。 (Migraine, external canthus pain in the eyes, chest and flank pain, swelling and pain under the armpits, athlete's foot, lower leg pain, lower limb paralysis, and sciatica.)
位置：在小腿外侧，当外踝尖上4寸，腓骨前缘稍前方。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the lateral side of the calf, 4 inches above the tip of the lateral ankle, slightly ahead of the anterior edge of the fibula.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
