所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：癫疾，瘈疭，膝股痛，胸胁胀满，膝踝肿痛，脚气，惊厥，下肢痿痹，胆囊炎，肋间神经痛，坐骨神经痛，腓肠肌痉挛等。 (Epilepsy, cramps, knee and tummy pain, chest and flanks, swelling and pain of knees and ankles, athlete's foot, convulsions, lower limb paralysis, cholecystitis, intercostal neuralgia, sciatica, gastrocnemius spasm, etc.)
位置：当小腿外侧，当外踝尖上7寸，腓骨后缘。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (When the lateral side of the calf, when the lateral ankle tip is 7 inches above the posterior edge of the fibula.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
