所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，眩晕，呕吐，小儿惊风，偏头痛，三叉神经痛，面神经麻痹，眩晕，顶骨部疼痛，胃炎，小儿高热惊厥。 (Headache, dizziness, vomiting, convulsions in children, migraines, trigeminal neuralgia, facial nerve paralysis, vertigo, parietal bone pain, gastritis, and high fever convulsions in children.)
位置：在头部，当耳尖直上入发际1.5寸，角孙直上方。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the head, when the tip of the ear is straight up to the hairline 1.5 inches, the corner is straight up to the top.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
