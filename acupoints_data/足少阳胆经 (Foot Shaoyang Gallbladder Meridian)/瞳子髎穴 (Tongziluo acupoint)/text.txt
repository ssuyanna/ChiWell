所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，目赤肿痛，迎风流泪，目翳，青盲，视力减退，结膜炎，泪囊炎，视神经萎缩等。 (Headache, red and swollen eyes, tears in the wind, eye cataracts, blue blindness, vision loss, conjunctivitis, dacryocystitis, optic nerve atrophy, etc.)
位置：在面部，目外眦旁，当眶外侧缘处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the face, beside the outer canthus, and at the outer edge of the orbit.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
