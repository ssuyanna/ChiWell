所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，目眩，癫痫，小儿惊风，颈项强痛，胸胁痛，半身不遂。 (Headache, dizziness, epilepsy, convulsions in children, strong neck pain, chest and flank pain, hemiplegia.)
位置：在头部，当前发际上0.5寸，神庭旁开3寸，神庭与头维连线的内三分与外三分交点处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the head, 0.5 inches on the hairline, 3 inches away from the Shenting, and at the intersection of the inner three-point and outer three-points of the line between the Shenting and the head dimension.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
