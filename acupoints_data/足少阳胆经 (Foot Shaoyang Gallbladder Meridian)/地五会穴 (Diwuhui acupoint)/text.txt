所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，目赤痛，耳鸣，耳聋，胸满，胁痛，腋肿，乳痈，跗肿。 (Headache, red eyes, tinnitus, deafness, chest fullness, flank pain, swelling axillary axilla, breast abscess, swelling of the tars.)
位置：在足背外侧，当足4趾本节（第4趾关节）的后方，第4、5趾骨之间，小趾伸肌腱的内侧缘。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the lateral side of the foot, behind the 4th toe section (4th toe joint), between the 4th and 5th phalangeal bones, the medial edge of the extensor tendon of the little toe.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
