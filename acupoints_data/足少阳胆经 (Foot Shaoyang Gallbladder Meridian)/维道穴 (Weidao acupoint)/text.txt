所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：腰胯痛，少腹痛，阴挺，疝气，带下，月经不调，水肿。 (Lower hip pain, lower abdominal pain, yin straightening, hernia, lowering of the belt, irregular menstruation, and edema.)
位置：在侧腹部，当髂前上棘的前下方，五枢前下0.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the side abdomen, when the anterior superior iliac spine is 0.5 inches lower than the five pivots.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
