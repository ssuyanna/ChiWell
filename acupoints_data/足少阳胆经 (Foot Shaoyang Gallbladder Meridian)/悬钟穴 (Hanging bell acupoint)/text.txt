所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：1. 颈项强痛，胸胁胀痛，落枕； 2. 痴呆，中风，偏头痛，半身不遂，腰腿疼痛，脚气，坐骨神经痛，下肢瘫痪等。 (1. Strong neck pain, chest and flank pain, stiff neck; 2. Dementia, stroke, migraine, hemiplegia, waist and legs pain, athlete's foot, sciatica, lower limb paralysis, etc.)
位置：在小腿外侧，当外踝尖上3寸，腓骨前缘。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the lateral side of the calf, 3 inches above the tip of the lateral ankle, the anterior edge of the fibula.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
