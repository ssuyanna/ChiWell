所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，眩晕，颈项强痛，胸胁痛，口苦，耳鸣，耳聋，耳痛，喉痹等。 (Headache, dizziness, strong neck pain, chest and flank pain, bitter mouth, tinnitus, deafness, ear pain, throat numbness, etc.)
位置：头部，当耳后乳突的后上方，天冲与完骨的中三分与下三分交点处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (The head is at the intersection of the middle and lower three-points of Tian Chong and Wan Gu.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
