所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：胸肋痛，喘息，呕吐，吞酸，腋肿，肩臂痛。 (Chest and rib pain, wheezing, vomiting, sore throat, armpit swelling, shoulder and arm pain.)
位置：在侧胸部，渊腋前1寸，平乳头，第4肋间隙中。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the side chest, 1 inch in front of the abyss, flat nipple, and 4th intercostal space.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
