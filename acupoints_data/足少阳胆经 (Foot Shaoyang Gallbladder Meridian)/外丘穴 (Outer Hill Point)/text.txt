所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：颈项强痛，胸胁痛，疯犬伤毒不出，下肢痿痹，癫疾，小儿龟胸，腿痛，脚气，胆囊炎，肝炎，坐骨神经痛等。 (Strong neck pain, chest and flank pain, mad dog injury and inability to expose poison, lower limb numbness, epilepsy, turtle chest pain in children, leg pain, athlete's foot, cholecystitis, hepatitis, sciatica, etc.)
位置：在小腿外侧，当外踝尖上7寸，腓骨前缘，平阳交穴。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the outside of the calf, 7 inches above the tip of the lateral ankle, the anterior edge of the fibula, and the Pingyang Jiao point.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
