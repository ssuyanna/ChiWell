所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，耳鸣，耳聋，聤耳，口眼喎斜，面痛，牙痛，惊痫，瘛疭。 (Headache, tinnitus, deafness, excessive ears, mouth and eyes, facial pain, toothache, epilepsy, and swelling.)
位置：位于耳前，下关直下，当颧弓的上缘凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located in front of the ear, directly below the lower gate, as the depression of the upper edge of the zygomatic arch.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
