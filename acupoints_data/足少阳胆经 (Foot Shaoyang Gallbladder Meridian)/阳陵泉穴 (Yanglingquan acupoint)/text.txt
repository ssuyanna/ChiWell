所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：胁肋痛，口苦，呕吐，黄疸，便秘，半身不遂，下肢痿痹，膝肿痛，肝炎，胆囊炎，胆道蛔虫症，坐骨神经痛，膝关节炎，小腿抽筋等。 (Flank pain, bitter mouth, vomiting, jaundice, constipation, hemiplegia, lower limb swelling, knee pain, hepatitis, cholecystitis, biliary ascariasis, sciatica, knee arthritis, calf cramps, etc.)
位置：在小腿外侧，当腓骨头前下方凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the lateral side of the calf, when the indentation is in front of the fibula.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
