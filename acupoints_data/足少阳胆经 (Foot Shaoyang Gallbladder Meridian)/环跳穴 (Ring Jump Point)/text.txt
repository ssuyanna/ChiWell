所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：腰腿痹痛，下肢痿痹，半身不遂，风疹，脚气，坐骨神经痛，髋关节疾患等。 (Lower leg pain, lower limb numbness, hemiplegia, rubella, athlete's foot, sciatica, hip joint disease, etc.)
位置：在股外侧部，侧卧屈股，当股骨大转子最凸点与骶管裂孔连线的外三分与中三分交点处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the lateral part of the femoral part, lie on the side and bend the thorax, when the intersection of the outer three-part and the middle three-part at the line connecting the most convex point of the femoral trochanter and the sacral canal fissure.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
