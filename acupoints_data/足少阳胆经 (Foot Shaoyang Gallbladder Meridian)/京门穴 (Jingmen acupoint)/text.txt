所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：腹胀，少腹痛，里急，泄泻，水道不通，溺黄，腰痛，骨痹痛引背，肾炎，肋间神经痛。 (Abdominal bloating, lower abdominal pain, internal urgency, diarrhea, obstruction of waterways, drowning, low back pain, bone pain leading to the back, nephritis, intercostal neuralgia.)
位置：在侧腰部，第十二肋游离端下方凹陷处，章门后1.8寸，后略平志室。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (At the side waist, the depression below the free end of the twelfth rib, 1.8 inches behind the gate, and the back is slightly flat in the room.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
