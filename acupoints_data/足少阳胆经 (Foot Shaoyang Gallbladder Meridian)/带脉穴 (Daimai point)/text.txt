所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：闭经，月经不调，赤白带下，腹痛，疝气，腰胁痛，子宫内膜炎，附件炎，盆腔炎，带状疱疹。 (Amenorrhea, irregular menstruation, red and leucorrhea, abdominal pain, hernia, low back pain, endometritis, adnexitis, pelvic inflammatory disease, shingles.)
位置：在侧腹部，当第11肋骨游离端下方垂线与脐水平线的交点上，肝经章门穴下1.8寸处；侧卧取穴。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the side abdomen, at the intersection of the vertical line and the umbilical horizontal line below the free end of the 11th rib, 1.8 inches below the Zhangmen point of the liver meridian; lie on the side to pick the point.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
