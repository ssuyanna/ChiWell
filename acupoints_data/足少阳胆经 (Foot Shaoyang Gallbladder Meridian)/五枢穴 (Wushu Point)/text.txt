所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：阴挺，赤白带下，月经不调，疝气，少腹痛，便秘，腰胯痛。 (Yin straighten, red and leucorrhea, irregular menstruation, hernia, lower abdominal pain, constipation, and waist and hip pain.)
位置：在侧腹部，当髂前上棘的前方，横平脐下3寸处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the side abdomen, in front of the anterior superior iliac spine, 3 inches horizontally below the navel.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
