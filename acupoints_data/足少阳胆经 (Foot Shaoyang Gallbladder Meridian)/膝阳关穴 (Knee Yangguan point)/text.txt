所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：膝肿痛，腘筋挛急，小腿麻木，膝关节炎，下肢瘫痪等。 (Swelling and pain in the knees, cramps of popliteal muscles, numbness of the calf, knee arthritis, paralysis of the lower limbs, etc.)
位置：在膝外侧，当阳陵泉上3寸，股骨外上髁上方的凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the lateral side of the knee, 3 inches above the Dangyanglingquan, the depression above the lateral epicondyle of the femoral.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
