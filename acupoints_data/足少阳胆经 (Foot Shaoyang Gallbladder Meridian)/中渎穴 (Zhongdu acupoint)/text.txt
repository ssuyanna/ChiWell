所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：半身不遂，下肢痿痹，脚气，腰膝痛，以及坐骨神经痛，中风后遗症等。 (Hemiplegia, lower limb numbness, athlete's foot, waist and knee pain, as well as sciatica, sequelae of stroke, etc.)
位置：在大腿外侧，腘横纹上5寸，当股外侧肌与肱二头肌之间，或于风市穴直下2寸处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the outside of the thigh, 5 inches above the popliteal transverse stripe, between the lateral femoral muscle and the biceps, or 2 inches below the Fengshi point.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
