所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，颈项强痛，目眩，目赤肿痛，鼻痛，耳鸣，癫痫，心悸，热病。 (Headache, strong neck pain, dizziness, red and swollen eyes, nasal pain, tinnitus, epilepsy, palpitations, fever.)
位置：位于人体的头部，当枕外隆凸的上缘外侧，头正中线旁开2.25寸，平脑户穴。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located on the head of the human body, on the outside of the upper edge of the occipital protrusion, 2.25 inches away from the midline of the head, Pingnaohu point.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
