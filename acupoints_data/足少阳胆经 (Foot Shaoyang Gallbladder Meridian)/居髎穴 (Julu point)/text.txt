所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：腰腿痹痛，月经不调，带下，疝气，及坐骨神经痛，下肢瘫痪等。 (Lower and leg pain, irregular menstruation, lower tractor, hernia, sciatica, lower limb paralysis, etc.)
位置：在髋部，当髂前上棘与股骨大转子最凸点连线的中点处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the hip, at the midpoint of the line connecting the anterior superior iliac spine to the femoral trochanter.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
