所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：偏头痛，面肿，目外眦痛，齿痛。 (Migraine, swelling of the face, pain in the outer canthus, and tooth pain.)
位置：在头部鬓发上，当头维与曲鬓弧形连线的中点处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the head and temples, at the midpoint of the curved line between the head dimension and the curvy temples.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
