所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：偏头痛，面肿，目外眦痛，耳鸣，上齿痛，三叉神经痛。 (Migraine, swelling of the face, external canthus pain in the eyes, tinnitus, upper toothache, trigeminal neuralgia.)
位置：在头部鬓发上，当头维与曲鬓弧形连线的上四分与下四分交点处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the head and temples, at the intersection of the upper and lower quarters of the head dimension and the curved temples.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
