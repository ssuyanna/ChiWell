所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：半身不遂，下肢痿痹、麻木，遍身瘙痒，脚气，坐骨神经痛，股外侧皮神经炎，荨麻疹等。 (Hemiplegia, lower limb numbness, itching throughout the body, athlete's foot, sciatica, lateral femoral dermatitis, urticaria, etc.)
位置：在大腿外侧部的中线上，当腘横纹上7寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the midline of the lateral thigh, 7 inches above the popliteal crossbone.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
