所属经脉：足少阳胆经 (Foot Shaoyang Gallbladder Meridian)
主调病症：偏头痛，目眩，目痛，耳聋，胁痛，失眠，多梦，热病，高血压，肋间神经痛等。 (Migraine, dizziness, eye pain, deafness, flank pain, insomnia, dreaming, fever, hypertension, intercostal neuralgia, etc.)
位置：在足第4趾末节外侧，距趾甲角0.1寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the outside of the end of the fourth toe of the foot, 0.1 inch from the toenail angle.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
