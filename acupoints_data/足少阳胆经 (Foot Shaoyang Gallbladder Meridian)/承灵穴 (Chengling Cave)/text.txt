主调病症：头晕，眩晕，目痛，鼻渊，鼻出血，鼻窒，多涕。 (Dizziness, dizziness, eye pain, abyss, nose bleeding, nose suffocation, and excessive snot.)
位置：在头部，当前发际上4寸，头正中线旁开2.25寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the head, the hairline is currently 4 inches, and the center line of the head is 2.25 inches away.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
