所属经脉：手少阴心经 (Hand Shaoyin Heart Sutra)
主调病症：心痛，胸闷，四肢不收，肩周炎，腋下瘰疬（颈淋巴结核），腋臭，悲愁不乐，咽干，烦渴，干呕，目黄，肩臂不举，肘臂挛痛，冠心病，心绞痛，心包炎，脑血管病后遗症，肋间神经痛，癔病，乳汁分泌不足。此外，弹拨本穴可预防冠心病及肺心病。 (Heartache, chest tightness, limb not retracting, shoulder periarthritis, underarm scrofula (cervical lymph node burr), armpit odor, sadness, dry throat, thirst, dry retching, yellow eyes, shoulder and arm do not lift, elbow and arm cramps, coronary heart disease, angina pectoris, pericarditis, sequelae of cerebrovascular disease, intercostal neuralgia, hysteria, insufficient milk secretion.In addition, plucking this acupoint can prevent coronary heart disease and pulmonary heart disease.)
位置：位于腋窝顶点，腋动脉搏动处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located at the axillary apex, at the pulsation of the axillary artery.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
