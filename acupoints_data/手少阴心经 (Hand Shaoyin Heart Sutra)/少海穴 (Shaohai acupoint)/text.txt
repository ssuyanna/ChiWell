所属经脉：手少阴心经 (Hand Shaoyin Heart Sutra)
主调病症：1. 精神神经系统疾病：神经衰弱，精神分裂症，头痛，眩晕，三叉神经痛，肋间神经痛，尺神经炎，尺神经麻痹； 2. 呼吸系统疾病：肺结核，胸膜炎； 3. 运动系统疾病：落枕，前臂麻木及肘关节周围软组织疾患，肘臂挛痛，下肢痿痹； 4. 其它：心绞痛，淋巴结炎，疔疮，瘰疬，急性舌骨肌麻痹或萎缩等。 (1. Mental and neurological diseases: neurasthenia, schizophrenia, headache, dizziness, trigeminal neuralgia, intercostal neuralgia, ulnar neuritis, ulnar nerve paralysis; 2. Respiratory diseases: tuberculosis, pleuritis; 3. Motor system diseases: stiff occipital, forearm numbness and soft tissue diseases around the elbow joints, elbow and arm cramps, lower limb paralysis; 4. Others: angina pectoris, lymphadenitis, boils, scrotum, acute hyoid muscle paralysis or atrophy, etc.)
位置：在肘横纹内侧端与肱骨内上髁连线的中点处，屈肘取穴。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (At the midpoint of the line connecting the medial end of the transverse elbow line to the medial epicondyle of the humerus, flex the elbow to select the acupoint.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
