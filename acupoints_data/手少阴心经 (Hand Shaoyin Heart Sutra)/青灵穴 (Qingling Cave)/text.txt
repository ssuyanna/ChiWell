所属经脉：手少阴心经 (Hand Shaoyin Heart Sutra)
主调病症：目黄，胁痛，腋下肿痛，肩臂不举等。 (Yellow eyes, flank pain, swollen and painful underarms, shoulders and arms not lifted, etc.)
位置：在臂内侧，当极泉与少海的连线上，肘横纹上3寸，肱二头肌的内侧沟中。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the inner side of the arm, on the line between Jiquan and Shaohai, 3 inches above the horizontal line of the elbow, in the medial groove of the biceps.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
