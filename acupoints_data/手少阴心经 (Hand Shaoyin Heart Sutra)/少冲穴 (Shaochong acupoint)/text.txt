所属经脉：手少阴心经 (Hand Shaoyin Heart Sutra)
主调病症：心悸，心痛，胸胁痛，昏迷，发热，癫狂，手挛臂痛等。 (Palpitations, heartache, chest and flank pain, coma, fever, madness, hand and arm pain, etc.)
位置：在手小指末节桡侧，距指甲角0.1寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the radial side of the end of the little finger, 0.1 inch from the nail angle.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
