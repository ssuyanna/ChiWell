所属经脉：手少阴心经 (Hand Shaoyin Heart Sutra)
主调病症：1. 心痛心烦，惊悸怔忡，失眠健忘，痴呆，癫狂，痫症等心与神志病证； 2. 高血压； 3. 胸胁痛，目黄，失音，喉痹，腕关节痛等。 (1. Heartache and annoyance, palpitations, insomnia and forgetfulness, dementia, madness, epilepsy and other heart and mental illness symptoms; 2. Hypertension; 3. Chest and flank pain, yellow eyes, loss of sound, throat paralysis, wrist joint pain, etc.)
位置：在腕部，腕掌侧横纹尺侧端，尺侧腕屈肌腱的桡侧凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (At the wrist, the transverse ulnar end of the wrist palm, and the radial depression of the flexor wrist tendon on the ulnar side.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
