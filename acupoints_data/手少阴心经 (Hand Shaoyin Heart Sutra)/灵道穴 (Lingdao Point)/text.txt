所属经脉：手少阴心经 (Hand Shaoyin Heart Sutra)
主调病症：心痛，失音，腕臂痛，肘臂挛痛，暴喑等。 (Heartache, loss of sound, wrist and arm pain, elbow and arm cramps, swelling, etc.)
位置：在前臂掌侧，当尺侧腕屈肌腱的桡侧缘，腕横纹上1.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the palm of the forearm, when the radial edge of the flexor wrist tendon on the ulnar side, 1.5 inches above the transverse wrist line.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
