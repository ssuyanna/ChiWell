所属经脉：手少阴心经 (Hand Shaoyin Heart Sutra)
主调病症：主治心痛，心悸，怔忡，暴喑，舌强不语，腕臂内侧痛，肘及前臂疼痛，咽喉肿痛，头痛，目眩，面赤热，遗尿，月经过多，狂症等。 (It is mainly used to treat heartache, palpitations, palpitations, violent silence, strong tongue and non-speaking, pain in the inner side of the wrist and arm, pain in the elbow and forearm, sore throat, headache, dizziness, red and hot facial features, enuresis, excessive menstrual periods, and craziness.)
位置：在前臂掌侧，当尺侧腕屈肌腱的桡侧缘，腕横纹上1寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the palm of the forearm, 1 inch above the radial edge of the flexor wrist tendon on the ulnar side.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
