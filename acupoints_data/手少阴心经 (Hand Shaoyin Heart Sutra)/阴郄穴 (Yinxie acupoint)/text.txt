所属经脉：手少阴心经 (Hand Shaoyin Heart Sutra)
主调病症：心痛，惊悸，盗汗，吐血，失音等。 (Heartache, palpitations, night sweats, vomiting of blood, loss of sound, etc.)
位置：在前臂掌侧，当尺侧腕屈肌腱的桡侧缘，腕横纹上0.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the palm of the forearm, when the radial edge of the flexor wrist tendon on the ulnar side, the transverse wrist line is 0.5 inches above.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
