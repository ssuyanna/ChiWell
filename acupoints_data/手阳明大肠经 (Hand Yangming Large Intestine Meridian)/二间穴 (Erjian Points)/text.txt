所属经脉：手阳明大肠经 (Hand Yangming Large Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：目昏，鼻出血，齿痛，牙龈炎，口歪，咽喉肿痛，热病，颔肿，面神经炎，三叉神经痛。 (Dizziness in eyes, nose bleeding, toothache, gingivitis, crooked mouth, sore throat, fever, swelling chin, facial neuritis, trigeminal neuralgia.)
位置：微握拳，在第二掌指关节前，桡侧凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Clench your fist slightly, in front of the second metacarpophalangeal joint, at the radial depression.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
