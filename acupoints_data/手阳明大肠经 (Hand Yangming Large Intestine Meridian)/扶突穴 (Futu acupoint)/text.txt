所属经脉：手阳明大肠经 (Hand Yangming Large Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽，气喘，咽喉肿痛，暴喑，瘰疬，瘿气。 (Cough, asthma, sore throat, swelling, scrotum, goiter.)
位置：位于人体的颈外侧部，结喉旁，当胸锁乳突肌前、后缘之间。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located on the lateral part of the human cervix, next to the throat, between the anterior and posterior edges of the sternocleidomastoid muscle.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
