所属经脉：手阳明大肠经 (Hand Yangming Large Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：发热，咽喉肿痛，目赤，齿痛，臂肘疼痛，上肢不遂，腹痛，吐泻，痢疾，癫狂，瘰疬，丹毒，疮疡，湿疹，荨麻疹，中暑，高血压，神经衰弱等。 (Fever, sore throat, red eyes, toothache, pain in the arms and elbows, abscess of upper limbs, abdominal pain, vomiting and diarrhea, dysentery, madness, scrofula, erysipelas, sores, eczema, urticaria, heat stroke, hypertension, neurasthenia, etc.)
位置：在肘横纹外侧端，屈肘，当尺泽与肱骨外上髁连线中点。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (At the lateral end of the transverse elbow, bend the elbow, and the midpoint of the line connecting Chize and the lateral epicondyle of the humerus.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
