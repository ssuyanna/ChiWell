所属经脉：手阳明大肠经 (Hand Yangming Large Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：1. 五官科系统疾病：牙痛，急性结膜炎，青光眼，齿痛，目痛； 2. 其它：三叉神经痛，扁桃体炎，手指肿痛，肩关节周围炎，咽喉肿痛，胸腹满，气喘，热病，手背红肿等。 (1. Ottologic system diseases: toothache, acute conjunctivitis, glaucoma, toothache, eye pain; 2. Others: trigeminal neuralgia, tonsillitis, swelling and painful fingers, peri-shoulder joint inflammation, sore throat, chest and abdomen, asthma, fever, red and swollen back of hands, etc.)
位置：位于手背第二掌骨桡侧，掌骨小头后方凹陷处，握拳取穴。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located on the radial side of the second metacarpal bone on the back of the hand, in the depression behind the small head of the metacarpal bone, clench the fist and select the acupoint.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
