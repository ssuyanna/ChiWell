所属经脉：手阳明大肠经 (Hand Yangming Large Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：1. 耳聋，青盲，齿痛，咽喉肿痛等五官疾患； 2. 中风昏迷，发热，颔肿，胸满，喘咳，手指麻木等。 (1. Deafness, blue blindness, toothache, sore throat and other facial features; 2. Stroke coma, fever, swelling chin, full chest, wheezing cough, numbness of fingers, etc.)
位置：在手食指末节桡侧，距指甲角0.1寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the radial side of the end of the index finger, 0.1 inch from the corner of the nail.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
