所属经脉：手阳明大肠经 (Hand Yangming Large Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：肘臂疼痛、拘挛、麻木、瘰疬等。 (Elbow and arm pain, cramps, numbness, scrotum, etc.)
位置：在臂外侧，屈肘，曲池上方1寸，当肱骨边缘处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the outside of the arm, bend the elbow, 1 inch above the flexion pond, at the edge of the humerus.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
