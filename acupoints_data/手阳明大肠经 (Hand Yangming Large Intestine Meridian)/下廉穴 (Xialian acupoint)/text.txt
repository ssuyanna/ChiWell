所属经脉：手阳明大肠经 (Hand Yangming Large Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：1. 运动系统疾病：网球肘，肘关节炎； 2. 消化系统疾病：腹痛，肠鸣音亢进； 3. 其它：头痛，目眩，目痛，急性脑血管病等。 (1. Sports system diseases: tennis elbow, elbow arthritis; 2. Digestive system diseases: abdominal pain, hypernethesia; 3. Others: headache, dizziness, eye pain, acute cerebrovascular disease, etc.)
位置：在前臂背面桡侧，当阳溪与曲池连线上，肘横纹下4寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the radial side of the forearm, on the line connecting Dangyangxi and Quchi, 4 inches below the horizontal line of the elbow.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
