所属经脉：手阳明大肠经 (Hand Yangming Large Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：1. 五官科系统疾病：口腔炎，舌炎，腮腺炎，口舌咽喉肿痛，口眼歪斜，扁桃体炎； 2. 其它：面神经麻痹，下腹壁肌肉痉挛，前臂疼痛，头痛，面肿，项强，肠鸣腹痛，疔疮等。 (1. Orthopaedic system diseases: stomatitis, glossitis, mumps, swollen and sore throat, crooked mouth and eyes, tonsillitis; 2. Others: facial nerve paralysis, lower abdominal wall muscle spasm, forearm pain, headache, face swelling, neck strength, intestinal rumbling and abdominal pain, boils, etc.)
位置：屈肘，在前臂背面桡侧，当阳溪与曲池连线上，腕横纹上5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Bend the elbow, on the radial side of the back of the forearm, on the line between Yangxi and Quchi, 5 inches above the horizontal line of the wrist.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
