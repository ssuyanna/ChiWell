所属经脉：手阳明大肠经 (Hand Yangming Large Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：1. 运动系统疾病：腰痛，肩臂痛，上肢麻痹，半身不遂； 2. 消化系统疾病：溃疡病，肠炎，消化不良，腹痛，吐泻； 3. 五官科系统疾病：牙痛，口腔炎； 4. 其它：颈淋巴结核，面神经麻痹，感冒，乳腺炎。 (1. Motor system diseases: low back pain, shoulder and arm pain, upper limb paralysis, hemiplegia; 2. Digestive system diseases: ulcer disease, enteritis, indigestion, abdominal pain, vomiting and diarrhea; 3. Oentreological system diseases: toothache, stomatitis; 4. Others: cervical lymph node tract, facial nerve paralysis, cold, mastitis.)
位置：在前臂背面桡侧，当阳溪与曲池连线上，肘横纹下2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the radial side of the forearm, on the line connecting Yangxi and Quchi, 2 inches below the horizontal line of the elbow.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
