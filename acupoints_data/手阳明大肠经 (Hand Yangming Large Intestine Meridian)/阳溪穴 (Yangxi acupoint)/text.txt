所属经脉：手阳明大肠经 (Hand Yangming Large Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：1. 五官科系统疾病：鼻炎，耳聋，耳鸣，目赤肿痛，齿痛，结膜炎，角膜炎； 2. 精神神经系统疾病：面神经麻痹，癫痫，精神病； 3. 其它：腕关节及周围软组织疾病，扁桃体炎，头痛，咽喉肿痛等。 (1. Oentangial system diseases: rhinitis, deafness, tinnitus, red and swollen eyes, toothache, conjunctivitis, keratitis; 2. Psychiatric neurological diseases: facial nerve paralysis, epilepsy, psychosis; 3. Others: wrist joint and surrounding soft tissue diseases, tonsillitis, headache, sore throat, etc.)
位置：在腕背横纹桡侧，手拇指向上翘起时，当拇短伸肌腱与拇长伸肌腱之间的凹陷中。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the radial side of the dorsal transverse line of the wrist, the thumb of the hand is raised upward, and in the depression between the extensor shortus tendon and the extensor longus tendon.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
