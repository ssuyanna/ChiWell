所属经脉：手阳明大肠经 (Hand Yangming Large Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，目眩，肠鸣腹痛，肩膊酸痛，手臂麻木，上肢不遂等。 (Headache, dizziness, intestinal rumbling and abdominal pain, shoulder pain, numbness in the arms, and upper limb abscess.)
位置：在前臂背面桡侧，当阳溪与曲池连线上，肘横纹下3寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the radial side of the forearm, on the line connecting Dangyangxi and Quchi, 3 inches below the horizontal line of the elbow.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
