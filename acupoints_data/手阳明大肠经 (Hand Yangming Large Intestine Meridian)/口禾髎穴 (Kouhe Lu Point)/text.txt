所属经脉：手阳明大肠经 (Hand Yangming Large Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：鼻塞，鼻衄，口歪，口噤。 (Nasal congestion, epistaxis, crooked mouth, and silence of mouth.)
位置：位于人体的上唇部，鼻孔外缘直下，平水沟穴。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located on the upper lip of the human body, directly down the outer edge of the nostril, Pingshuigou point.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
