所属经脉：手阳明大肠经 (Hand Yangming Large Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：肩臂疼痛，颈项强急，瘿气，瘰疬，及肩关节周围炎，急性结膜炎等。 (Shoulder and arm pain, strong and urgent neck, goiter, scrotum, shoulder joint periarthritis, acute conjunctivitis, etc.)
位置：在臂外侧，三角肌止点处，当曲池与肩髃连线上，曲池上7寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the outside of the arm, at the end of the deltoid muscle, when the Quchi connects the shoulders, the Quchi is 7 inches above the Quchi.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
