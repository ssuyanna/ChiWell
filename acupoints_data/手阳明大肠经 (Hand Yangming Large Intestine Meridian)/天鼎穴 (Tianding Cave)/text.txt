所属经脉：手阳明大肠经 (Hand Yangming Large Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：暴喑气梗，咽喉肿痛，瘰疬，瘿气。 (Swelling and sore throat, scrotum, goiter.)
位置：位于人体的颈外侧部，胸锁乳突肌后缘，当结喉旁，扶突穴与缺盆穴连线中点。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located on the lateral part of the human cervix, the posterior edge of the sternocleidomastoid muscle, next to the throat, the midpoint of the line connecting the Futu point and the pelvic cervix.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
