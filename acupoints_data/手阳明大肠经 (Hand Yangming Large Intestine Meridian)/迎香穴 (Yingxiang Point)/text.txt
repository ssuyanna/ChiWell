所属经脉：手阳明大肠经 (Hand Yangming Large Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：鼻塞，鼻出血，鼻渊，鼻息肉，口眼歪斜，面痒浮肿，及胆道蛔虫症等。 (Nasal congestion, nasal bleeding, nasal abyss, nasal polyps, crooked mouth and eyes, itchy and swelling of the face, and biliary ascariasis.)
位置：在鼻翼外缘中点旁，当鼻唇沟中。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Next to the midpoint of the outer edge of the nose, when in the nasolabial groove.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
