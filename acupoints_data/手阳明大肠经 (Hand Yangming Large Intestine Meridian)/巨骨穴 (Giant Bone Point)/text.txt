所属经脉：手阳明大肠经 (Hand Yangming Large Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：肩背疼痛，半身不遂，瘾疹，瘰疬，以及肩关节周围炎等。 (Shoulder and back pain, hemiplegia, rash, scrotum, and periarthritis of the shoulder joint.)
位置：在肩上部，当锁骨肩峰端与肩胛冈之间凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the upper shoulder, when the depression between the acromion of the clavicle and the scapula.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
