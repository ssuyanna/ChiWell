所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽，气喘，胸痛，不嗜食等。 (Cough, asthma, chest pain, no appetite, etc.)
位置：在胸部，当第1肋间隙，前正中线旁开2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the chest, when the first intercostal space is 2 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
