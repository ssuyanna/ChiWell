所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：阴部痛，子宫脱垂，遗精，带下，月经不调，阴挺，痛经，不妊，泄泻，痢疾，阳痿，早泄，膀胱疾病等。 (Pupil pain, uterine prolapse, nocturnal emission, irregular menstruation, yin protrusion, dysmenorrhea, infertility, diarrhea, dysentery, impotence, premature ejaculation, bladder diseases, etc.)
位置：在下腹部，当脐中下4寸，前正中线旁开0.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the lower abdomen, 4 inches below the navel, 0.5 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
