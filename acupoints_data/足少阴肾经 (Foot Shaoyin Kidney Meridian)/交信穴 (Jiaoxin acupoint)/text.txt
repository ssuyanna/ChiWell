所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：月经不调，崩漏，带下，阴挺，阴痒，闭经，泄泻，五淋，泻痢赤白，便秘，痢疾，疝气，睾丸肿痛等。 (Irregular menstruation, melancholy, lower uterine bleeding, yin itching, amenorrhea, diarrhea, five rays, diarrhea, red and white diarrhea, constipation, dysentery, hernia, swelling and painful testicles, etc.)
位置：在小腿内侧，当太溪直上2寸，复溜前0.5寸，胫骨内侧缘的后方。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the inner side of the calf, when Taixi is straight up 2 inches, 0.5 inches in front of Fuliu, and behind the medial edge of the tibia.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
