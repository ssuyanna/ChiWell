所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：腹痛，腹胀，呕吐，消化不良，心痛，心悸，胸痛，暴喑。 (Abdominal pain, bloating, vomiting, indigestion, heartache, palpitations, chest pain, storm.)
位置：在上腹部，当脐中上5寸，前正中线旁开0.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the upper abdomen, 5 inches above the middle of the navel, 0.5 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
