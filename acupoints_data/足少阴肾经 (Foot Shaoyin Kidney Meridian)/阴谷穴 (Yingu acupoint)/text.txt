所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：月经不调，崩漏，带下，疝气，阴囊湿痒，阴中痛，癫狂，膝股内侧痛，小便不利，遗精，阳痿，及尿路感染，尿潴留等。 (Irregular menstruation, membranaceus, underwear, hernia, dampness and itchy scrotum, pain in the vagina, madness, pain in the knee and internal hem, difficulty urinating, sperm, impotence, urinary tract infection, urinary retention, etc.)
位置：在腘窝内侧，屈膝时，当半腱肌肌腱与半膜肌肌腱之间。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the medial side of the popliteal fossa, when the knee is bent, when the semi-tenentendosus tendon is between the semimembranous tendon and the semimembranous tendon.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
