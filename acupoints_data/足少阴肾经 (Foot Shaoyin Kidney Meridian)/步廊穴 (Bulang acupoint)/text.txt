所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：胸痛，咳嗽，气喘，呕吐，不嗜食，乳痈。 (Chest pain, cough, asthma, vomiting, no appetite, and breast abscess.)
位置：在胸部，当第5肋间隙，前正中线旁开2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the chest, when the 5th intercostal space is separated by 2 inches.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
