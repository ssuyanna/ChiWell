所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：癫狂，痫证，小腿内侧痛，腹痛，呕吐，疝气，脚软无力，腓肠肌痉挛，肾炎，睾丸炎，盆腔炎等。 (Epilepsy syndrome, epilepsy syndrome, internal calf pain, abdominal pain, vomiting, hernia, weakness in the feet, gastrocnemius spasm, nephritis, orchitis, pelvic inflammatory disease, etc.)
位置：在小腿内侧，当太溪与阴谷的连线上，太溪上5寸，腓肠肌肌腹的内下方。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the inner side of the calf, on the connection between Taixi and Yingu, 5 inches above Taixi, inside and below the gastrocnemius muscle abdomen.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
