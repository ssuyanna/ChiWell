所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：1. 泌尿生殖系统疾病：肾炎，睾丸炎，尿路感染； 2. 精神神经系统疾病：小儿麻痹后遗症，脊髓炎； 3. 消化系统疾病：腹胀，水肿，肠鸣，泄泻； 4. 其它：功能性子宫出血，腹膜炎，痔疮，腰肌劳损，盗汗，自汗，脚气，腿肿，足痿。 (1. Urinary and reproductive system diseases: nephritis, orchitis, urinary tract infection; 2. Psychiatric neurological diseases: sequelae of polio, myelitis; 3. Digestive system diseases: abdominal distension, edema, intestinal rumbling, diarrhea; 4. Others: functional uterine bleeding, peritonitis, hemorrhoids, lumbar muscle strain, night sweats, spontaneous sweating, athlete's foot, swelling of the legs, and foot impotence.)
位置：在小腿内侧，太溪直上2寸，跟腱的前方。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the inner side of the calf, Taixi is 2 inches straight up, in front of the Achilles tendon.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
