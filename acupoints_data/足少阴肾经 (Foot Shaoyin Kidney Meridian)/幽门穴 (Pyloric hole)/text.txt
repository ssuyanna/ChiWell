所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：胸胁背相引痛，恶心，呕吐，饮食不下，善哕，心痛，逆气，烦心，胃痉挛，胃下垂，肋间神经痛，腹痛，消化不良，泄泻，痢疾。 (Chest, flank and back pain, nausea, vomiting, dietary inability, good at ease, heartache, reversal of qi, restlessness, stomach cramps, stomach sagging, intercostal neuralgia, abdominal pain, indigestion, diarrhea, and dysentery.)
位置：在上腹部脐上六寸（巨阙）旁开五分处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Five minutes away from the upper abdomen (Juque) umbilical.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
