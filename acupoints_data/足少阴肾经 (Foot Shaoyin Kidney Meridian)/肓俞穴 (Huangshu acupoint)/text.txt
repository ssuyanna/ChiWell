所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：腹痛，呕吐，腹胀，痢疾，泄泻，便秘，疝气，月经不调，腰脊痛，小便淋沥等。 (Abdominal pain, vomiting, abdominal distension, dysentery, diarrhea, constipation, hernia, irregular menstruation, lumbar spinal pain, urination, etc.)
位置：在腹中部，当脐中旁开0.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the middle of the abdomen, 0.5 inches apart from the middle of the navel.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
