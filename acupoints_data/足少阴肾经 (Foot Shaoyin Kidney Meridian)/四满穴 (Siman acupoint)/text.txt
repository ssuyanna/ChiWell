所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：月经不调，崩漏，带下，不孕，产后恶露不净，小腹痛，遗精，遗尿，疝气，便秘，水肿。 (Irregular menstruation, melancholy, erosion, infertility, impurity of postpartum lochia, lower abdomen pain, nocturnal sperm, enuresis, hernia, constipation, and edema.)
位置：位于人体的下腹部，当脐中下2寸，前正中线旁开0.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located in the lower abdomen of the human body, 2 inches below the navel, 0.5 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
