所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：胸胁支满，咳嗽，气喘，乳痛，肋间神经痛，呕吐，不嗜食等。 (Full of chest and flank branches, cough, asthma, breast pain, intercostal neuralgia, vomiting, and not wanting to eat.)
位置：在胸部，当第4肋间隙，前正中线旁开2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the chest, when the 4th intercostal space is separated by 2 inches from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
