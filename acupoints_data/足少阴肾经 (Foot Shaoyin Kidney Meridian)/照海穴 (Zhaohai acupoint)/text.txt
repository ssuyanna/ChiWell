所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：1. 痫证，失眠等精神、神志疾患； 2. 咽干咽痛，目齿肿痛等五官热性病证； 3. 小便不利，小便频数； 4. 月经不调，赤白带下，阴挺，阴痒，痛经等妇科病证； 5. 下肢痿痹。 (1. Epilepsy syndrome, mental and mental illnesses such as insomnia; 2. Dry throat and sore throat, swollen and painful eyes and teeth, and other fever symptoms of five senses; 3. Difficulty urination and frequent urination; 4. Irregular menstruation, red and leucorrhea, yin protrusion, yin itching, dysmenorrhea and other gynecological symptoms; 5. Lower limb weakness.)
位置：在足内侧，内踝尖下方凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the medial side of the foot, in the depression below the tip of the medial ankle.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
