所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽，气喘，胸痛，烦满，呕吐，不嗜食，肋间神经痛等。 (Cough, asthma, chest pain, fullness, vomiting, no appetite, intercostal neuralgia, etc.)
位置：在胸部，当第二肋间隙，前正中线旁开2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the chest, when the second intercostal space is separated by 2 inches.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
