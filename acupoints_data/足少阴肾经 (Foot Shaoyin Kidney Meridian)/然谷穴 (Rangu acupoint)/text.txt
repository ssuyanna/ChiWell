所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：1. 月经不调，带下，阴挺； 2. 遗精，阳痿，小便不利； 3. 咯血，咽喉肿痛； 4. 消渴； 5. 小儿脐风，口噤不开； 6. 下肢痿痹，足跗痛。 (1. Irregular menstruation, lowering, and yin protrusion; 2. Nocturnal emission, impotence, and difficulty in urination; 3. Hemoptysis, sore throat; 4. Diagnosis; 5. Navel wind in children, inability to open the mouth; 6. Lower limb numbness, foot tarsal pain.)
位置：在足内侧缘，足舟骨粗隆下方，赤白肉际。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the medial edge of the foot, below the trochondrhea of the foot, the red and white flesh.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
