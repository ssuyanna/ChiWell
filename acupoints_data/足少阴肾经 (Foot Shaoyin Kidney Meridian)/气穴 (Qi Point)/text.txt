所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：月经不调，闭经，痛经，不孕，带下，小便不通，泄泻，痢疾，腰脊痛，阳痿，腰部疼痛，冷感症等。 (Irregular menstruation, amenorrhea, dysmenorrhea, infertility, leucorrhea, diarrhea, dysentery, lumbar spinal pain, impotence, lumbar pain, cold sensation, etc.)
位置：在下腹部，当脐中下3寸，前正中线旁开0.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the lower abdomen, 3 inches below the navel, 0.5 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
