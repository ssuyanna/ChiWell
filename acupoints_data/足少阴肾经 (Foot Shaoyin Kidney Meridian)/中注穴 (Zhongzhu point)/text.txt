所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：月经不调，腹痛，泄泻，便秘，腰腹疼痛，大便燥结，小便淋沥等。 (Irregular menstruation, abdominal pain, diarrhea, constipation, pain in the waist and abdomen, dry and stumps, dripping urine, etc.)
位置：在下腹部，当脐中下1寸，前正中线旁开0.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the lower abdomen, 1 inch below the mid-normal line, 0.5 inch away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
