所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：月经不调，痛经，闭经，阴挺，小便不利，目视昏花等。 (Irregular menstruation, dysmenorrhea, amenorrhea, yin protrusion, urinary discomfort, dizziness in vision, etc.)
位置：在足内侧，内踝后下方，当太溪直下1寸，跟骨结节的内侧凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the medial side of the foot, behind the medial ankle, 1 inch down from Taixi, the medial depression of the calcaneal tuberculosis.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
