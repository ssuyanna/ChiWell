所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：腹胀，肠鸣，腹痛，便秘，妇人不孕，胸胁满，疟疾，胃痛，呕吐，泄泻等。 (Abdominal bloating, intestinal rumbling, abdominal pain, constipation, infertility in women, chest and flank fullness, malaria, stomach pain, vomiting, diarrhea, etc.)
位置：在上腹部，当脐中上4寸，前正中线旁开0.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the upper abdomen, 4 inches above the middle of the navel, 0.5 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
