所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：阴部痛，小腹痛，遗精，阳痿，遗尿，疝气，小便不利，尿闭，睾丸肿痛，尿失禁等。 (Pupil pain, lower abdomen pain, nocturnal emission, impotence, enuresis, hernia, difficulty urination, urinary closure, swelling and painful testicles, urinary incontinence, etc.)
位置：在下腹部，当脐中下5寸，前正中线旁开0.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the lower abdomen, 5 inches below the mid-normal line, 0.5 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
