所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：腹胀，腹痛，食欲不振，泄泻，便秘等。 (Abdominal bloating, abdominal pain, loss of appetite, diarrhea, constipation, etc.)
位置：在上腹部，当脐中上2寸，前正中线旁开0.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the upper abdomen, 2 inches above the middle of the navel, 0.5 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
