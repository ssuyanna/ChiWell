所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽，气喘，呕吐，不嗜食，胸痛等。 (Cough, asthma, vomiting, no appetite, chest pain, etc.)
位置：在胸部，当锁骨下缘，前正中线旁开2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the chest, when the lower edge of the clavicle, 2 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
