所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：1. 精神神经系统疾病：神经衰弱，精神病，痴呆，癔病； 2. 泌尿生殖系统疾病：尿潴留，淋病； 3. 其它：哮喘，咽痛，口腔炎，咳血，食道狭窄，便秘，疟疾，嗜卧，足跟痛等。 (1. Mental and neurological diseases: neurasthenia, psychiatric disorders, dementia, hysteria; 2. Urinary and reproductive diseases: urinary retention, gonorrhea; 3. Others: asthma, sore throat, stomatitis, coughing up blood, esophageal stenosis, constipation, malaria, lying down, heel pain, etc.)
位置：在足内侧，内踝后下方，当跟腱附着部的内侧前方凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the medial side of the foot, behind the medial malleolus, as the medial anterior depression of the Achilles tendon attachment.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
