所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽，气喘，痰多，胸胁胀痛，呕吐，乳痈，肋间神经痛等。 (Cough, asthma, excessive phlegm, chest and flank swelling, vomiting, breast abscess, intercostal neuralgia, etc.)
位置：在胸部，当第3肋间隙，前正中线旁开2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the chest, when the third intercostal space is separated by 2 inches.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
