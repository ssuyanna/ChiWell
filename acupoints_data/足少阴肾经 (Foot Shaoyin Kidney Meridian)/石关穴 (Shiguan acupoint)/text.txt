所属经脉：足少阴肾经 (Foot Shaoyin Kidney Meridian)
主调病症：腹痛，呕吐，呃逆，便秘，妇人不孕等。 (Abdominal pain, vomiting, hiccups, constipation, infertility in women, etc.)
位置：在上腹部，当脐中上3寸，前正中线旁开0.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the upper abdomen, 3 inches above the middle of the navel, 0.5 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
