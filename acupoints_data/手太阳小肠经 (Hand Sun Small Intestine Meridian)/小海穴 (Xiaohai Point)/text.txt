所属经脉：手太阳小肠经 (Hand Sun Small Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，耳鸣，项强，颊肿，瘰疬，癫痫，肘臂痛等。 (Headache, tinnitus, neck strength, swelling of cheeks, scrofula, epilepsy, elbow and arm pain, etc.)
位置：在肘内侧，当尺骨鹰嘴与肱骨内上髁之间凹陷处，屈肘取穴。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the medial side of the elbow, when the depression between the ulna eagle beak and the medial epicondyle of the humerus, flex the elbow to select the acupoint.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
