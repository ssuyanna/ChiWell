所属经脉：手太阳小肠经 (Hand Sun Small Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：头痛项强，落枕，目赤肿痛，耳聋，耳鸣，鼻衄，癫痫，疟疾，黄疸，盗汗，腰背腿痛，肘、臂、手指挛急等。 (Headache, strong neck, stiff neck, red and swollen eyes, deafness, tinnitus, epilepsy, epilepsy, malaria, jaundice, night sweats, pain in the waist, back and legs, cramps in the elbow, arms, and fingers.)
位置：在手掌尺侧，微握拳，当第5掌指关节后的远侧掌横纹头赤白肉际处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the side of the palm of the hand, clench the fist slightly, and the red and white fleshy part of the distal palm line behind the 5th metacarpal joint.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
