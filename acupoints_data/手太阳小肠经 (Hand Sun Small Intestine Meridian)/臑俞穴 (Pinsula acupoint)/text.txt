所属经脉：手太阳小肠经 (Hand Sun Small Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：肩臂疼痛，瘰疬。 (Shoulder and arm pain, scrofula.)
位置：位于人体的肩部，当腋后纹头直上，肩胛冈下缘凹陷中。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located on the shoulder of the human body, when the back of the armpit is straight up, the lower edge of the shoulder scapula is sunken.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
