所属经脉：手太阳小肠经 (Hand Sun Small Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：1. 精神神经系统疾病：脑血管病后遗症，肩臂部神经痛； 2. 运动系统疾病：急性腰扭伤，落枕； 3. 其它：目视不明，肩、背、肘、臂臑痛等。 (1. Psychiatric neurological diseases: sequelae of cerebrovascular disease, shoulder and arm neuralgia; 2. Motor system diseases: acute lumbar sprain, stiff neck; 3. Others: unclear vision, shoulder, back, elbow, arm pain, etc.)
位置：位于前臂伸侧，腕后1寸，以掌向胸时，当尺骨茎突桡侧缘上方之骨缝中。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located on the forearm, 1 inch behind the wrist, and facing the chest with the palm, it is in the bone crease above the radial edge of the ulnar styloid process.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
