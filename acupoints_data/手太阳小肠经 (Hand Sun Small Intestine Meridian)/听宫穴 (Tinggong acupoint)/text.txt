所属经脉：手太阳小肠经 (Hand Sun Small Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：耳鸣，耳聋，聤耳，聋哑，癫狂，齿痛，神经性耳聋，中耳炎，外耳道炎，三叉神经痛、头痛、目眩头昏。 (Tinnitus, deafness, hyperactivity, deafness, madness, toothache, neurodeafness, otitis media, otitis external otitis, trigeminal neuralgia, headache, dizziness and dizziness.)
位置：在面部，耳屏前，下颌骨髁状突的后方，张口时呈凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the face, in front of the tragus, behind the condyle-shaped process of the mandible, it is a depression when opening the mouth.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
