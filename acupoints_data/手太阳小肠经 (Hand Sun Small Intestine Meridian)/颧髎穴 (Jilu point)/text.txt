所属经脉：手太阳小肠经 (Hand Sun Small Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：口眼歪斜，眼睑动，齿痛，颊肿。 (The mouth and eyes are crooked, the eyelids are moved, the toothache, and the cheeks are swollen.)
位置：在面部，当目外眦直下，颧骨下缘凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the face, directly below the outer canthus of the eye, and the depression of the lower edge of the zygomatic bone.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
