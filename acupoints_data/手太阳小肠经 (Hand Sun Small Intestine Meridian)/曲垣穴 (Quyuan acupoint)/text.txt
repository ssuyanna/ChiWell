所属经脉：手太阳小肠经 (Hand Sun Small Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：肩背疼痛，颈项强急，冈上肌腱炎，肩胛部拘挛疼痛，肩关节周围软组织疾病。 (Shoulder and back pain, strong and urgent neck, supraspinatus tendonitis, scapula cramps pain, and soft tissue diseases around the shoulder joint.)
位置：在肩胛部，冈上窝内侧端，当臑俞与第2胸椎棘突连线的中点处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (At the scapula, the medial end of the supra fossa, at the midpoint of the line connecting the spinous process of the second thoracic vertebra.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
