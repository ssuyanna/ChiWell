所属经脉：手太阳小肠经 (Hand Sun Small Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：1. 乳痈，乳汁少等乳疾； 2. 昏迷，热病等急证、热证； 3. 头痛，鼻出血，目翳，头痛，项强，咽喉肿痛等头面五官病证。 (1. Breast abscess, low milk, and other breast diseases; 2. Emergency symptoms and heat symptoms such as coma, fever, etc.; 3. Headache, nose bleeding, eye cataracts, headache, strong neck, sore throat and other head and face.)
位置：在手小指末节尺侧，距指甲角0.1寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the side of the last part of the little finger, 0.1 inch from the corner of the nail.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
