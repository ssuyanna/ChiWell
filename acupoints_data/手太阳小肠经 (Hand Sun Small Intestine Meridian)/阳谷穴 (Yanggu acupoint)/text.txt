所属经脉：手太阳小肠经 (Hand Sun Small Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：1. 精神神经系统疾病：精神病，癫痫，肋间神经痛，尺神经痛； 2. 五官科系统疾病：头痛目眩，目赤肿痛，神经性耳聋，耳鸣，口腔炎，齿龈炎， 腮腺炎； 3. 颌肿，腕关节疾患等。 (1. Psychiatric neurological diseases: psychiatric, epilepsy, intercostal neuralgia, ulnar neuralgia; 2. Oentreological system diseases: headache, dizziness, red and swollen eyes, neurodeafness, tinnitus, stomatitis, gingivitis, mumps; 3. Swelling jaw, wrist joint diseases, etc.)
位置：在手腕尺侧，当尺骨茎突与三角骨之间的凹陷中。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the ulnar side of the wrist, in the depression between the ulnar styloid process and the triangular bone.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
