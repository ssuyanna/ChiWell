所属经脉：手太阳小肠经 (Hand Sun Small Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：耳鸣，耳聋，咽喉肿痛，颈项强痛。 (Tinnitus, deafness, sore throat, and strong neck pain.)
位置：下颌角后，胸锁乳突肌前缘。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (After the mandibular angle, the anterior edge of the sternocleidomastoid muscle.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
