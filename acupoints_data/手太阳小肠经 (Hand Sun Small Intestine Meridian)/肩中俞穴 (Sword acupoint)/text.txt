所属经脉：手太阳小肠经 (Hand Sun Small Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：1. 呼吸系统疾病：支气管炎，哮喘，支气管扩张，吐血； 2. 其它：项强，视力减退，肩背疼痛等。 (1. Respiratory diseases: bronchitis, asthma, bronchodilation, vomiting of blood; 2. Others: neck strength, vision loss, shoulder and back pain, etc.)
位置：在背部，在第7颈椎棘突下，旁开2寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back, under the spinous process of the 7th cervical vertebra, 2 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
