所属经脉：手太阳小肠经 (Hand Sun Small Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：耳鸣，耳聋，咽喉肿痛，颈项强痛，暴喑。 (Tinnitus, deafness, sore throat, strong neck pain, and sluggishness.)
位置：位于人体的颈外侧部，胸锁乳突肌的后缘，扶突穴后，与喉结相平。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located on the outer part of the human cervix, the posterior edge of the sternocleidomastoid muscle, behind the Futu point, it is level with the Adam's apple.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
