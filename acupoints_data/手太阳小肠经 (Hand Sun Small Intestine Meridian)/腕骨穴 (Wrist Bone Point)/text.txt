所属经脉：手太阳小肠经 (Hand Sun Small Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，项强，耳鸣耳聋，目翳，肩臂疼痛麻木，腕痛，指挛，胁痛，热病汗不出，口腔炎，黄疸，消渴，糖尿病，瘛疭，惊风，疟疾。 (Headache, neck strength, tinnitus, deafness, eye cataracts, shoulder and arm pain, wrist pain, finger cramps, flank pain, fever and sweating, stomatitis, jaundice, diabetes, swelling, convulsions, malaria.)
位置：在手掌尺侧，当第5掌骨基底与钩骨之间，赤白肉际凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the ulnar side of the palm, between the base of the fifth metacarpal bone and the hook bone, the red and white flesh joints are sunken.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
