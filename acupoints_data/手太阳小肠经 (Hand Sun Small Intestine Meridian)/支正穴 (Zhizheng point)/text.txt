所属经脉：手太阳小肠经 (Hand Sun Small Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：头痛，项强，目眩，颌肿，癫狂，消渴，肘挛，指痛等。 (Headache, neck strength, dizziness, swelling of jaws, madness, diabetes, elbow cramps, finger pain, etc.)
位置：在前臂背面尺侧，当阳谷与小海的连线上，腕背横纹上5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the ulnar side of the forearm, on the connection between Dangyang Valley and Xiaohai, the horizontal line on the back of the wrist is 5 inches above.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
