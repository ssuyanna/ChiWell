所属经脉：手太阳小肠经 (Hand Sun Small Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：热病无汗，头痛项强，耳聋，耳鸣，目赤，鼻塞，咽痛，痄腮，产后无乳，手指麻木等。 (Fever diseases have no sweat, headache, strong neck, deafness, tinnitus, red eyes, nasal congestion, sore throat, cheeks, postpartum breasts, numbness, etc.)
位置：在小指尺侧，第五掌指关节前方，掌指横纹端凹陷处，赤白肉际，握拳取穴。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the side of the little finger, in front of the fifth palm joint, in the depression at the horizontal end of the palm and finger, the red and white flesh, clench the fist to select the acupoint.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
