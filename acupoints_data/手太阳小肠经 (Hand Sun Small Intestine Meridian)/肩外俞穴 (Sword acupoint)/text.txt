所属经脉：手太阳小肠经 (Hand Sun Small Intestine Meridian)
主调病症：1. 运动系统疾病：肩背酸痛，颈项强急，落枕，颈椎病，肩胛区神经痛，痉挛，麻痹； 2. 其它：肺炎，胸膜炎，神经衰弱，低血压等。 (1. Motor system diseases: shoulder and back pain, strong and urgent neck, stiff neck, cervical spondylosis, scapula, neuralgia, spasm, paralysis; 2. Others: pneumonia, pleuritis, neurasthenia, hypotension, etc.)
位置：在背部，当第1胸椎棘突下，旁开3寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the back, when the spinous process of the first thoracic vertebra is 3 inches apart.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
