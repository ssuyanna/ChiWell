所属经脉：足厥阴肝经 (Jueyin liver meridian)
主调病症：月经不调，带下，外阴搔痒，股内侧痛，下肢麻痹。（配曲骨穴、次髎穴、三阴交穴调理湿热下注之月经不调、白带多、阴门搔痒、股癣等；配肾俞穴、大赫穴、命门穴、太溪穴调理妇人不孕、男子不育症；配委中穴、次髎穴、膀胱俞穴调理膀胱炎、膀胱结石） (Irregular menstruation, lower tractor, itchy vulva, pain in the medial thigh, and paralysis of the lower limbs.(Combined with Qugu point, Cixu point, Sanyinjiao point to regulate damp heat, irregular menstruation, excessive leucorrhea, tinea thigh, tinea thigh, etc.; matched with Shenshu point, Dahe point, Mingmen point, Taixi point to regulate women's infertility and male infertility; matched with Weizhong point, Cixu point to regulate cystitis and bladder stones))
位置：在大腿内侧，当气冲直下2寸，大腿根部，耻骨结节的下方，长收肌的外缘。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the inner side of the thigh, when the qi rushes 2 inches down, at the base of the thigh, below the pubic tubercle, the outer edge of the long-term contractile muscle.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
