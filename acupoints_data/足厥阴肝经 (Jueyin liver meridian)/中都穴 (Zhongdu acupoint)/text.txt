所属经脉：足厥阴肝经 (Jueyin liver meridian)
主调病症：月经不调，崩漏，带下，疝气，少腹痛，遗精，泄痢等。 (Irregular menstruation, melanoma, underwear, hernia, lower abdomen pain, nocturnal sperm, diarrhea, etc.)
位置：在小腿内侧，当足内踝尖上7寸，胫骨内侧面的中央。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the inner side of the calf, 7 inches above the tip of the medial ankle, the center of the medial side of the tibia.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
