所属经脉：足厥阴肝经 (Jueyin liver meridian)
主调病症：1. 头痛、眩晕、目赤肿痛、青盲、口喎等头面五官病证； 2. 中风，癫痫，小儿惊风； 3. 黄疸、胁痛、呕逆、腹胀等肝胃病证； 4. 月经不调、痛经、闭经、带下等妇科病证； 5. 遗尿，癃闭； 6. 下肢痿痹，足跗肿痛； 7. 消气，可缓解人因生气引起的一些疾病。 (1. Headache, dizziness, red and swollen eyes, blue blindness, oral paralysis and other symptoms of head and face; 2. Stroke, epilepsy, convulsion in children; 3. Hepatostomia, flank pain, vomiting, abdominal distension and other liver and stomach diseases; 4. Gynecological symptoms such as irregular menstruation, dysmenorrhea, amenorrhea, and lower lesions; 5. Enuresis, closure; 6. Lower limb numbness, swollen and painful feet; 7. Relieve breath, can relieve some diseases caused by people's anger.)
位置：在足背侧，在第1跖骨间隙的后方凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the dorsal side of the foot, at the posterior depression of the first metatarsal space.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
