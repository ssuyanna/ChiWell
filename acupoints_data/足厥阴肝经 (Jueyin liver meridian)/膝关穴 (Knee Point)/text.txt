所属经脉：足厥阴肝经 (Jueyin liver meridian)
主调病症：膝膑肿痛，寒湿走注，历节风痛，下肢痿痹，脚气，鹤膝风等。 (Swelling and pain of knee stomatology, cold and dampness, wind pain in Lijie, lower limb numbness, athlete's foot, and knee wind.)
位置：在小腿内侧，当胫骨内上髁的后下方，阴陵泉后1寸，腓肠肌内侧头的上部。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the inner side of the calf, when the posterior and bottom of the medial epicondyle of the tibia, 1 inch behind the Yinlingquan, the upper part of the medial head of the gastrocnemius muscle.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
