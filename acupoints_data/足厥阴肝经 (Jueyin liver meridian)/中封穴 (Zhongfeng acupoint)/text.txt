所属经脉：足厥阴肝经 (Jueyin liver meridian)
主调病症：疝气，阴茎痛，遗精，小便不利，黄疸，胸腹胀满，腰痛，足冷，内踝肿痛。 (Hernia, penis pain, nocturnal emission, difficulty urinating, jaundice, chest and abdominal distension, low back pain, cold feet, swollen and painful inner ankle.)
位置：在足背侧，当足内踝前，商丘与解溪连线之间，胫骨前肌腱的内侧凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the dorsal side of the foot, in front of the medial ankle of the foot, between the line connecting Shangqiu and Jiexi, the medial depression of the anterior tibial tendon.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
