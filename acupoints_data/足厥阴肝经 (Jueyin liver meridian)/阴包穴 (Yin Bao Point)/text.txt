所属经脉：足厥阴肝经 (Jueyin liver meridian)
主调病症：1.泌尿生殖系统疾病：月经不调，盆腔炎，遗尿，小便不利； 2.其他疾病：腰腿痛，骶髂关节炎，腰肌劳损，腹股沟淋巴结炎。 (1. Urinary and reproductive system diseases: irregular menstruation, pelvic inflammatory disease, enuresis, and difficulty in urination; 2. Other diseases: lumbar and leg pain, sacroiliac arthritis, lumbar muscle strain, inguinal lymphadenitis.)
位置：在大腿内侧，当股骨内上髁上4寸，股内肌与缝匠肌之间。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the medial thigh, 4 inches above the medial epicondyle of the femur, between the internal femur and the sartois muscle.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
