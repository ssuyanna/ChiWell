所属经脉：足厥阴肝经 (Jueyin liver meridian)
主调病症：胸胁胀满疼痛，呕吐，黄疸，呃逆，吞酸，腹胀，泄泻，饥不欲食，胸中热，喘咳，奔豚，疟疾，伤寒热入血室。 (Thoracic and flank bloating pain, vomiting, jaundice, hiccups, acid swallowing, abdominal distension, diarrhea, hunger and lack of appetite, chest heat, asthma and cough, running pig, malaria, typhoid fever enters the blood chamber.)
位置：在胸部，当乳头直下，第6肋间隙，前正中线旁开4寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the chest, when the nipple is straight down, the 6th intercostal space is 4 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
