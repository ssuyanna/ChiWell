所属经脉：足厥阴肝经 (Jueyin liver meridian)
主调病症：腹痛，腹胀，肠鸣，泄泻，呕吐，神疲肢倦，胸胁痛，黄疸，痞块，小儿疳积，腰脊痛。 (Abdominal pain, abdominal distension, intestinal rumbling, diarrhea, vomiting, mental fatigue, limb fatigue, chest and flank pain, jaundice, lumps, malnutrition in children, and lumbar spinal pain.)
位置：该穴位于人体的侧腹部，当第11肋游离端的下方。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (This point is located on the side abdomen of the human body, below the free end of the 11th rib.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
