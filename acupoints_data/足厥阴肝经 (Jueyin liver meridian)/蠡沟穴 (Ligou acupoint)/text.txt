所属经脉：足厥阴肝经 (Jueyin liver meridian)
主调病症：胫部酸痛，月经不调，阴痒，崩漏，带下，阴挺，疝气，睾丸肿痛，子宫内膜炎，子宫脱垂，小便不利，遗精，足胫酸痛等。 (Tibia ache, irregular menstruation, vaginal itching, melancholy, underbelly, vaginal erectile dysfunction, hernia, swelling and painful testicles, endometritis, uterine prolapse, difficulty in urination, nocturnal sperm, sore feet and shin.)
位置：在小腿内侧，当足内踝尖上5寸，胫骨内侧面的中央。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the inner side of the calf, 5 inches above the tip of the medial ankle, the center of the medial side of the tibia.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
