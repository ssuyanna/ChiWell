所属经脉：足厥阴肝经 (Jueyin liver meridian)
主调病症：疝气，阴挺，阴茎痛，月经不调，少腹痛，股内侧痛。 (Hernia, erectile slight penis, penis pain, irregular menstruation, lower abdominal pain, and medial thigh pain.)
位置：在耻骨结节的外侧，当气冲外下方腹股沟股动脉搏动处，前正中线旁开2.5寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the lateral side of the pubic tuberculosis, when the qi rushes outwardly at the pulsation of the inguinal and femoral artery, it is 2.5 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
