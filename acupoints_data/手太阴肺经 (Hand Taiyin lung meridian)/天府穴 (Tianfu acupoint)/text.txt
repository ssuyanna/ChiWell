所属经脉：手太阴肺经 (Hand Taiyin lung meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽，喘息，鼻衄，瘿气，上臂前外侧痛等。 (Cough, wheezing, epistaxis, goiter, anterior and external pain on the upper arm, etc.)
位置：在臂内侧面，肱二头肌桡侧缘，腋前纹头下3寸处，布有臂外侧皮神经，肌皮神经和头静脉，肱动、静脉肌支。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the medial side of the arm, the radial edge of the biceps brachii, 3 inches below the anterior axillary head, there are lateral arm cutic nerves, myodermal nerves and cephalic veins, brachial artery and venous muscle branches.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
