所属经脉：手太阴肺经 (Hand Taiyin lung meridian)
主调病症：1. 呼吸系统疾病：支气管炎，肺炎，咳嗽，哮喘，肺结核，支气管扩张； 2. 运动系统疾病：胸膺痛，肩背痛，肩关节周围软组织损伤如肩周炎。 (1. Respiratory system diseases: bronchitis, pneumonia, cough, asthma, tuberculosis, bronchodilation; 2. Motor system diseases: chest pain, shoulder and back pain, soft tissue damage around the shoulder joint such as periarthritis of the shoulder.)
位置：在胸前壁的外上方，云门下1寸，平第1肋间隙，距前正中线6寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Above the outer wall of the chest, 1 inch below the Yunmen, level the first intercostal gap, 6 inches from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
