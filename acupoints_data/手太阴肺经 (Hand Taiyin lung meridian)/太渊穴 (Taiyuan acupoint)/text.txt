所属经脉：手太阴肺经 (Hand Taiyin lung meridian)
主调病症：呼吸系统疾病：咳嗽，气喘，咯血，咽痛，扁桃体炎，肺炎； 循环系统疾病：心动过速，无脉症，脉管炎； 其它：肋间神经痛，桡腕关节及周围软组织疾患，膈肌痉挛。 (Respiratory system diseases: cough, asthma, hemoptysis, sore throat, tonsillitis, pneumonia; circulatory system diseases: tachycardia, pulselessness, vasculitis; Others: intercostal neuralgia, radial wrist joint and surrounding soft tissue diseases, diaphragm spasm.)
位置：在腕掌侧横纹桡侧，桡动脉搏动处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the radial side of the transverse line on the wrist and palm, the radial artery pulsation.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
