所属经脉：手太阴肺经 (Hand Taiyin lung meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽，气喘，支气管炎，支气管哮喘，肺炎，心动过速，上臂痛。 (Cough, asthma, bronchitis, bronchial asthma, pneumonia, tachycardia, upper arm pain.)
位置：在臂内侧面，肱二头肌桡侧缘，腋前纹头下4寸，或肘横纹上5寸处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the inner side of the arm, the radial edge of the biceps brachii, 4 inches below the anterior axillary head, or 5 inches above the horizontal elbow line.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
