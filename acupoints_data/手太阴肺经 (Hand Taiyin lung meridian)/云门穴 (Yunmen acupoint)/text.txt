所属经脉：手太阴肺经 (Hand Taiyin lung meridian)
主调病症：1. 呼吸系统疾病：咳嗽、气管炎、胸痛、哮喘； 2. 其它：胸痛，肩背痛，肩关节周围炎。 (1. Respiratory diseases: cough, bronchitis, chest pain, asthma; 2. Others: chest pain, shoulder and back pain, and peri-shoulder joint inflammation.)
位置：在胸前壁的外上方，肩胛骨喙突上方，锁骨下窝凹陷处，距前正中线6寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Above the outer wall of the chest, above the coracoid process of the scapula, the depression of the subclavian fossa, 6 inches from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
