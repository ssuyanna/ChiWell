所属经脉：手太阴肺经 (Hand Taiyin lung meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽，咯血，咽喉肿痛，失音，发热，扁桃体炎，小儿疳积，小儿营养不良等。 (Cough, hemoptysis, sore throat, loss of sound, fever, tonsillitis, malnutrition in children, etc.)
位置：第1掌骨中点桡侧，赤白肉际处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (The radial side of the middle point of the first metacarpal bone, at the red and white flesh.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
