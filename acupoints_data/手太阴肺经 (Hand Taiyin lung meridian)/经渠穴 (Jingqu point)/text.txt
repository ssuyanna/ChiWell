所属经脉：手太阴肺经 (Hand Taiyin lung meridian)
主调病症：1. 呼吸系统疾病：气管炎，支气管炎，哮喘，肺炎，扁桃体炎，发热，胸痛； 2. 精神神经系统疾病：膈肌痉挛，食道痉挛，桡神经痛或麻痹。 (1. Respiratory system diseases: bronchitis, bronchitis, asthma, pneumonia, tonsillitis, fever, chest pain; 2. Psychiatric neurological diseases: diaphragm spasm, esophageal spasm, radial neuralgia or paralysis.)
位置：在前臂掌面桡侧，桡骨茎突与桡动脉之间凹陷处，腕横纹上1寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the radial side of the forearm, the depression between the radial styloid process and the radial artery, 1 inch above the transverse wrist line.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
