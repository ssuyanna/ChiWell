所属经脉：手太阴肺经 (Hand Taiyin lung meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽，气喘，咳血，鼻衄，潮热，咽喉肿痛，喑哑，胸胁胀满，心烦，乳痈，肘臂挛痛，肩内侧痛，上肢不遂，手不能伸，胃痛，腹痛，急性吐泻，中暑，口舌干渴，发热，丹毒，小儿惊风，抽搐，绞肠痧，小便频急，淋沥涩痛，心痛，无脉症，肺炎，支气管炎，支气管哮喘，肺结核，急性胃肠炎，肘关节及周围软组织疾患等。 (Cough, asthma, coughing up blood, epistaxis, hot flashes, sore throat, dumbness, chest and flanks, restlessness, breast abscess, elbow and arm cramps, medial shoulder pain, upper limb abscess, hands cannot stretch, stomach pain, abdominal pain, acute vomiting and diarrhea, heat stroke, mouth and tongue thirst, fever, erysipelas, convulsions in children, convulsions, clogging intestines, frequent urination, pain of swelling, heartache, pulselessness, pneumonia, bronchitis, bronchial asthma, tuberculosis, acute gastroenteritis, elbow joints and surrounding soft tissue diseases, etc.)
位置：在肘横纹中，肱二头肌腱桡侧凹陷处，微屈肘取穴。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the transverse elbow line, the elbow is slightly bent to select the acupoint.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
