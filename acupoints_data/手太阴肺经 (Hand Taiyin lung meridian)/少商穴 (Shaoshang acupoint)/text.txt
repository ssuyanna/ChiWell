所属经脉：手太阴肺经 (Hand Taiyin lung meridian)
主调病症：发热，昏迷，咳嗽，咽喉肿痛，癫狂，鼻衄等。 (Fever, coma, cough, sore throat, madness, epilepsy, etc.)
位置：在手拇指末节桡侧，距指甲角0.1寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the radial side of the end of the thumb, 0.1 inch from the corner of the nail.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
