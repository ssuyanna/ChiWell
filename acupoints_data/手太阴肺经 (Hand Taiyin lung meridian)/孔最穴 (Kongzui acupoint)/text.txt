所属经脉：手太阴肺经 (Hand Taiyin lung meridian)
主调病症：1. 咳血，咳嗽，气喘，咽喉肿痛等肺系病证； 2. 肘臂挛痛，痔疾。 (1. Coughing blood, coughing, asthma, sore throat and other lung diseases; 2. Crisps of elbow and arm, hemorrhoids.)
位置：在前臂掌面桡侧，当尺泽与太渊连线上，腕横纹上7寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the radial side of the forearm, on the line connecting Chize and Taiyuan, the horizontal line of the wrist is 7 inches above.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
