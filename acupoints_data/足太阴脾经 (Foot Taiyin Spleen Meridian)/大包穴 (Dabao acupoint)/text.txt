所属经脉：足太阴脾经 (Foot Taiyin Spleen Meridian)
主调病症：气喘，哮喘，胸闷，心内膜炎，胸膜炎，肋间神经痛，胸胁病等呼吸系统疾病，全身疼痛，四肢无力，食多身瘦。 (Asthma, asthma, chest tightness, endocarditis, pleurisitis, intercostal neuralgia, chest and flank disease and other respiratory diseases, general pain, weakness in limbs, and body weight loss.)
位置：在侧胸部，腋中线上，当第6肋间隙处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the side chest, mid-axillary line, when the 6th intercostal space.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
