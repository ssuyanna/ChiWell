所属经脉：足太阴脾经 (Foot Taiyin Spleen Meridian)
主调病症：胃痛，腹胀，呕吐，泄泻，便秘，热病无汗，手足逆冷等。 (Stomach pain, abdominal distension, vomiting, diarrhea, constipation, fever, no sweating, cold hands and feet, etc.)
位置：在足内侧缘，当足大趾本节（第1跖趾关节）前下方赤白肉际凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the medial edge of the foot, the red and white flesh-like depression in front of this section of the big toe (the first metatarsophalal joint).(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
