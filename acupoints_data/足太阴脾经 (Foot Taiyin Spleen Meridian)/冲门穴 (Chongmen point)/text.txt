所属经脉：足太阴脾经 (Foot Taiyin Spleen Meridian)
主调病症：1. 泌尿生殖系统疾病：尿潴留，睾丸炎，精索神经痛，小便淋沥，尿闭； 2. 妇产科系统疾病：子痫，子宫内膜炎，乳腺炎，带下，乳少； 3. 其它：腹痛，胃肠痉挛，疝气。 (1. Urinary and reproductive system diseases: urinary retention, orcheitis, spermatocord neuralgia, urinary stagnation, urinary closure; 2. Obstetric and gynecological system diseases: eclampsia, endometriitis, mastitis, underbelly, breast loss; 3. Others: abdominal pain, gastrointestinal cramps, hernia.)
位置：在腹股沟外侧，距耻骨联合上缘中点3.5寸，当髂外动脉搏动处的外侧。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the lateral side of the inguinal groove, 3.5 inches from the midpoint of the upper edge of the pubic joint, when the pulsation of the external iliac artery.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
