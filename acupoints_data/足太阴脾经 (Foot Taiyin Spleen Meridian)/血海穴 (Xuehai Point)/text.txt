所属经脉：足太阴脾经 (Foot Taiyin Spleen Meridian)
主调病症：月经不调，闭经，痛经，崩漏，功能性子宫出血，阴部痒痛，带下，产后恶露不尽，贫血，睾丸炎，小便淋涩，气逆，腹胀，风疹，瘾疹，湿疹，皮肤瘙痒，神经性皮炎，丹毒，股内侧痛，膝关节疼痛等。 (Irregular menstruation, amenorrhea, dysmenorrhea, melancholy, functional uterine bleeding, itching and pain in the vagina, incomplete postpartum lochia, anemia, orchitis, astringent urination, reversal of qi, abdominal distension, rubella, addiction, eczema, skin itching, neurodermatitis, erysipelas, medial femoral pain, knee joint pain, etc.)
位置：在大腿内侧，髌底内侧端上2寸，当股四头肌内侧头的隆起处；屈膝取穴。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the inner thigh, 2 inches above the inner end of the patellar basal end, and at the bulge of the inner head of the quadriceps femoris; bend the knee to select the acupoint.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
