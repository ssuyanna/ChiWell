所属经脉：足太阴脾经 (Foot Taiyin Spleen Meridian)
主调病症：1. 腹胀、腹泻、水肿、黄疸、小便不利或失禁等脾不运化水湿病证； 2. 遗精，月经不调，赤白带下，膝胫酸痛等。 (1. Symptoms of spleen failure to transport water and dampness such as abdominal distension, diarrhea, edema, jaundice, difficulty in urination or incontinence; 2. Nocturnal emission, irregular menstruation, red and leucorrhea, knee and shin pain, etc.)
位置：在小腿内侧，当胫骨内侧髁后下方凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the medial calf, the depression behind the medial condyle of the tibia.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
