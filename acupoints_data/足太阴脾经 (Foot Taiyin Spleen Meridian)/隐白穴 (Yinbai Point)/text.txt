所属经脉：足太阴脾经 (Foot Taiyin Spleen Meridian)
主调病症：1. 月经过多，带下，崩漏等妇科病证； 2. 便血，尿血等慢性出血证； 3. 癫狂，惊风，多梦等神志疾患； 4. 腹胀，泄泻。 (1. Gynecological symptoms such as menstrual period, escaping, membranaceus, etc.; 2. Chronic bleeding symptoms such as bloody stools and hematuria; 3. Constant disorders such as madness, convulsions, and dreams; 4. Abdominal bloating and diarrhea.)
位置：在足大趾末节内侧，距趾甲角0.1寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the inside of the end of the big toe of the foot, 0.1 inch from the toenail angle.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
