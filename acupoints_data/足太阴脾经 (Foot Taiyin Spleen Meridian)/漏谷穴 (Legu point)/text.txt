所属经脉：足太阴脾经 (Foot Taiyin Spleen Meridian)
主调病症：腹胀，肠鸣，消化不良，小便不利，丹毒，脚气，偏坠，腿膝厥冷，遗精，下肢痿痹等。 (Abdominal bloating, intestinal rumbling, indigestion, difficulty in urination, erysipelas, athlete's foot, falling, cold legs and knees, nocturnal sperm, lower limb numbness, etc.)
位置：在小腿内侧，当内踝尖与阴陵泉的连线上，距内踝尖6寸，胫骨内侧缘后方。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the inner side of the calf, on the line between the tip of the medial ankle and Yinlingquan, 6 inches away from the tip of the medial ankle, behind the medial edge of the tibia.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
