所属经脉：足太阴脾经 (Foot Taiyin Spleen Meridian)
主调病症：胃痛，腹胀，肠鸣，泄泻，黄疸，便秘，神经性呕吐，消化不良，急、慢性胃炎，急慢性肠炎，腓肠肌痉挛，乳腺疾病，感冒后恶心呕吐，踝关节及周围软组织疾患等。 (Stomach pain, abdominal distension, intestinal rumbling, diarrhea, jaundice, constipation, neuronal vomiting, indigestion, acute and chronic gastritis, acute and chronic enteritis, gastrocnemius spasm, breast diseases, nausea and vomiting after a cold, diseases of the ankle joint and surrounding soft tissues, etc.)
位置：在足内踝前下方凹陷中，当舟骨结节与内踝尖连线的中点处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the anterior and lower depression of the medial ankle, at the midpoint of the line connecting the scaphoid tuberculosis with the medial ankle tip.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
