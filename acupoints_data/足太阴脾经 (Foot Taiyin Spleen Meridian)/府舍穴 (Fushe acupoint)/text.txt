所属经脉：足太阴脾经 (Foot Taiyin Spleen Meridian)
主调病症：腹痛，积聚，痞块，疝气等。 (Abdominal pain, accumulation, lumps, hernia, etc.)
位置：在下腹部，当脐中下4寸，冲门上方0.7寸，距前正中线4寸处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (In the lower abdomen, 4 inches below the navel, 0.7 inches above the door, 4 inches from the front midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
