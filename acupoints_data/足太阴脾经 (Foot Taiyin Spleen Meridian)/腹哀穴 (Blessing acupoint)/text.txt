所属经脉：足太阴脾经 (Foot Taiyin Spleen Meridian)
主调病症：腹痛，泄泻，便秘，痢疾，消化不良等。 (Abdominal pain, diarrhea, constipation, dysentery, indigestion, etc.)
位置：位于腹部脐上3寸。前正中线旁开4寸处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located 3 inches above the abdomen.4 inches away from the front center line.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
