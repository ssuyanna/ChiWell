所属经脉：足太阴脾经 (Foot Taiyin Spleen Meridian)
主调病症：1. 月经不调，痛经，崩漏等妇科病症； 2. 腹痛，腹胀，腹泻，食欲不振等脾胃病症； 3. 小便不利，水肿等等脾不运化水湿病症。 (1. Gynecological diseases such as irregular menstruation, dysmenorrhea, membranaceus, etc.; 2. Spleen and stomach diseases such as abdominal pain, abdominal distension, diarrhea, loss of appetite; 3. Spleen and stomach problems such as urination, edema, etc.)
位置：在小腿内侧，当内踝尖与阴陵泉的连线上，阴陵泉下3寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the inner side of the calf, on the line between the tip of the inner ankle and Yinlingquan, 3 inches below Yinlingquan.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
