所属经脉：足太阴脾经 (Foot Taiyin Spleen Meridian)
主调病症：小便不利，遗溺，鼠蹊肿痛，阴囊湿痒，腹股沟肿痛等。 (Discomfortable urination, drowning, swollen and painful groin, damp and itchy scrotum, swelling and painful groin, etc.)
位置：在大腿内侧，当血海与冲门连线上，血海上6寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the inner side of the thigh, when the sea of blood is connected to the gate, the sea of blood is 6 inches above the sea of blood.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
