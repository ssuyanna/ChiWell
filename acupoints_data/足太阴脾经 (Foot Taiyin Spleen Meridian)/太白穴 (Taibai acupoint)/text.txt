所属经脉：足太阴脾经 (Foot Taiyin Spleen Meridian)
主调病症：胃痛，腹胀，呕吐，呃逆，肠鸣，泄泻，痢疾，便秘，脚气，痔漏等。 (Stomach pain, abdominal distension, vomiting, hiccups, intestinal rumbling, diarrhea, dysentery, constipation, athlete's foot, hemorrhoids, etc.)
位置：在足内侧缘，当足大趾本节（第1跖趾关节）后下方赤白肉际凹陷处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the medial edge of the foot, the red and white flesh-like depression behind this section of the big toe (the first metatarsophalal joint).(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
