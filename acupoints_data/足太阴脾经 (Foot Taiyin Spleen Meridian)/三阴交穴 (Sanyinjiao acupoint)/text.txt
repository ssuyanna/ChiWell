所属经脉：足太阴脾经 (Foot Taiyin Spleen Meridian)
主调病症：脾胃虚弱，消化不良，腹胀肠鸣，腹泻，月经不调，崩漏，带下，闭经，子宫脱垂，难产，产后血晕，恶露不行，遗精，阳痿，睾丸缩腹，阴茎肿痛，水肿，小便不利，遗尿，膝脚痹痛，脚气，失眠，湿疹，荨麻疹，神经性皮炎，高血压病等。 (Weakness of spleen and stomach, indigestion, abdominal distension and intestinal rumbling, diarrhea, irregular menstruation, melancholy, underwear, amenorrhea, uterine prolapse, difficult delivery, postpartum blood dizziness, lochia, inability to do so, nocturnal emission, impotence, testicles contraction, penis swollen and painful, edema, urinary urination, enuresis, knee pain, athlete's foot, insomnia, eczema, urticaria, neurodermatitis, hypertension, etc.)
位置：位于内踝尖直上三寸，胫骨后缘。 （此穴为双穴，左右各一） (Located three inches above the medial ankle tip, posterior edge of the tibia.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
