所属经脉：足太阴脾经 (Foot Taiyin Spleen Meridian)
主调病症：1. 呼吸系统疾病：气管炎，肺炎，胸膜炎，肋间神经痛； 2. 其它：肝炎，腹胀水肿，尿潴留，噫气，反胃。 (1. Respiratory diseases: bronchitis, pneumonia, pleuritis, intercostal neuralgia; 2. Others: hepatitis, abdominal distension and edema, urinary retention, benign, and nausea.)
位置：在胸外侧部，当第5肋间隙，距前正中线6寸处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the lateral side of the chest, when the 5th intercostal space is 6 inches away from the anterior midline.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
