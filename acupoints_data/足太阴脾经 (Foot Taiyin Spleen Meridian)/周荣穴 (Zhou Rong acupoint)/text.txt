所属经脉：足太阴脾经 (Foot Taiyin Spleen Meridian)
主调病症：咳嗽，气喘，胸胁胀满，饮食不下等。 (Cough, asthma, chest and flank, and dietary inadequate.)
位置：在胸外侧部，当第2肋间隙，距前正中线6寸。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the lateral side of the chest, when the second intercostal space is 6 inches away from the anterior median line.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
