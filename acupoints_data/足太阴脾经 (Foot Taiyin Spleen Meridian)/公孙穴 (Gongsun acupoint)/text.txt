所属经脉：足太阴脾经 (Foot Taiyin Spleen Meridian)
主调病症：1. 胃痛，呕吐，腹痛，腹泻，痢疾，肠鸣，食不化等胃肠病证； 2. 心烦，失眠，狂证等神志病证； 3. 逆气里急，气上冲心（奔豚气）等冲脉病证； 4. 脚气等。 (1. Gastrointestinal diseases such as stomach pain, vomiting, abdominal pain, diarrhea, dysentery, intestinal rumbling, and inability to eat; 2. Conscious diseases such as restlessness, insomnia, and crazy symptoms; 3. Conscious diseases such as reverse qi and rushing to the heart (running to the pig qi) and other Chongmai diseases such as Chongmai; 4. Athlete, etc.)
位置：在足内侧缘，当第1跖骨基底的前下方，赤白肉际处。（此穴为双穴，左右各一） (On the medial edge of the foot, at the anterior and lower part of the first metatarsal base, at the red and white flesh.(This acupoint is a double acupoint, one on the left and right))
