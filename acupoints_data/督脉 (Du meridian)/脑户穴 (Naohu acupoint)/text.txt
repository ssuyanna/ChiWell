所属经脉：督脉 (Du meridian)
主调病症：头重，头痛，面赤，目黄，眩晕，面痛、音哑，项强，癫狂痫证，舌本出血，瘿瘤，失眠，枕神经痛等。 (Heavy head, headache, red face, yellow eyes, dizziness, facial pain, hoarse voice, strong neck, epilepsy syndrome, tongue bleeding, goiter, insomnia, occipital neuralgia, etc.)
位置：在头部，后发际正中直上2.5寸，风府上1.5寸，枕外隆凸的上缘凹陷处。 (On the head, 2.5 inches straight up the middle of the posterior hairline, 1.5 inches above the Fengfu, and the depression at the upper edge of the protruding bulge of the pillow.)
