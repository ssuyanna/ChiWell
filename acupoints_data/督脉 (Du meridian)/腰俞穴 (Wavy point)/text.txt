所属经脉：督脉 (Du meridian)
主调病症：腰脊强痛，腹泻，便秘，痔疾，脱肛，便血，癫痫，淋浊，月经不调，下肢痿痹。 (Strong pain in the waist and spine, diarrhea, constipation, hemorrhoids, prolapse of the anus, bloody stools, epilepsy, turbidity, irregular menstruation, and paralysis of the lower limbs.)
位置：在骶部，当后正中线上，适对骶管裂孔。 (In the sacral part, on the posterior median line, it is suitable for the sacral canal fissure.)
