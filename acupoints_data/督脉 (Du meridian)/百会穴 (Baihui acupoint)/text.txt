所属经脉：督脉 (Du meridian)
主调病症：头痛，头重脚轻，痔疮，高血压，低血压，宿醉，目眩失眠，焦躁，阳气不足，神经衰弱等。 (Headache, heavy head and light feet, hemorrhoids, high blood pressure, hypotension, hangover, dizziness, insomnia, anxiety, insufficient yang energy, neurasthenia, etc.)
位置：在头顶正中线与两耳尖连线的交点处。 (At the intersection of the center line of the head and the tips of the ears.)
