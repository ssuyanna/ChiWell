所属经脉：督脉 (Du meridian)
主调病症：健忘，惊悸，咳嗽，疟疾，脊背强痛，癫痫，神经衰弱，肋间神经痛，晕眩，情绪不安，红脸症等。 (Forgetfulness, palpitations, cough, malaria, strong spinal pain, epilepsy, neurasthenia, intercostal neuralgia, dizziness, mood restlessness, face redness, etc.)
位置：在背部，当后正中线上，第5胸椎棘突下凹陷中。 (On the back, on the posterior median line, the 5th thoracic vertebra is in the depression below the spinous process.)
