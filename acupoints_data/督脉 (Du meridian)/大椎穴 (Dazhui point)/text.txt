所属经脉：督脉 (Du meridian)
主调病症：发热，疟疾，中暑，感冒，气喘，癫狂，癫痫，骨蒸潮热，盗汗，脊背强急，项强，肺结核，支气管炎，颈椎病，头痛，小儿惊风，痤疮。 (Fever, malaria, heat stroke, cold, asthma, epilepsy, bone steaming hot flashes, night sweats, strong spine, neck strength, tuberculosis, bronchitis, cervical spondylosis, headache, convulsions in children, acne.)
位置：在后背正中线上，第七颈椎棘突下凹陷中。 (On the midline of the dorsal, it is in the depression below the spinous process of the seventh cervical vertebra.)
