所属经脉：督脉 (Du meridian)
主调病症：胸胁胀痛，脊强，腰背疼痛，黄疸，胆囊炎，胆道蛔虫症，胃肠炎，肋间神经痛，咳嗽，气喘，肝炎，疟疾等。 (Chest and flank swelling, strong spine, back pain, jaundice, cholecystitis, biliary ascariasis, gastroenteritis, intercostal neuralgia, cough, asthma, hepatitis, malaria, etc.)
位置：在背部，当后正中线上，第7胸椎棘突下凹陷中。 (On the back, on the posterior median line, the 7th thoracic vertebra is in the depression below the spinous process.)
