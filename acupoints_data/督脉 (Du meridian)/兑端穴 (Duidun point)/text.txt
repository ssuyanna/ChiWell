所属经脉：督脉 (Du meridian)
主调病症：昏迷，晕厥，癫狂，癔病，消渴嗜饮，口疮臭秽，口噤，鼻塞，齿龈痛，鼻出血，鼻炎。 (Coma, fainting, madness, hysteria, diabetes, appetite for drinking, mouth sores, foul mouth, nose congestion, pain in the gums, nose bleeding, rhinitis.)
位置：位于人体的面部，当上唇的尖端，人中沟下端的皮肤与唇的移行部。 (Located on the human face, the tip of the upper lip, the lower end of the human midrib, and the moving part of the lips.)
