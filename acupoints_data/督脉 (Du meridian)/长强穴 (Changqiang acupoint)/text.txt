所属经脉：督脉 (Du meridian)
主调病症：痔疮，脱肛，便血，便秘，遗精，遗尿，腹泻，痢疾，腰背强痛，癫痫，及精神分裂症，前列腺炎等。 (Hemorrhoids, prolapse of the anus, bloody stool, constipation, nocturnal sperm, enuresis, diarrhea, dysentery, strong back pain, epilepsy, schizophrenia, prostatitis, etc.)
位置：在尾骨端下，当尾骨端与肛门连线的中点处。 (Below the coccyx end, at the midpoint of the line connecting the coccyx end to the anus.)
