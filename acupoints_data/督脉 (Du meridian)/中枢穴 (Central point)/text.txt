所属经脉：督脉 (Du meridian)
主调病症：胃痛，呕吐，腹胀满，食欲不振，黄疸，腰背痛，肝炎，胆囊炎等。 (Stomach pain, vomiting, abdominal distension, loss of appetite, jaundice, low back pain, hepatitis, cholecystitis, etc.)
位置：在背部，当后正中线上，当第10胸椎棘突下凹陷中。 (On the back, on the posterior median line, when the 10th thoracic vertebra is sunken under the spinous process.)
