所属经脉：督脉 (Du meridian)
主调病症：癫狂，惊痫，抽搐，脊强，背痛，胃痛，黄疸，四肢不收，筋挛拘急。 (Crazy, epilepsy, convulsions, strong spine, back pain, stomach pain, jaundice, limbs not retracted, and muscle cramps are tight.)
位置：在背部，当后正中线上，第9胸椎棘突下凹陷中。 (On the back, on the posterior median line, the 9th thoracic vertebra is in the depression below the spinous process.)
