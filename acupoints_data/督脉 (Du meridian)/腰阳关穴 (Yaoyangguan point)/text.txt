所属经脉：督脉 (Du meridian)
主调病症：1. 腰骶疼痛，下肢痿痹； 2. 月经不调、赤白带下等妇科病证； 3. 遗精、阳痿等男科病证。 (1. Lumbar sacral pain, lower limb impotence; 2. Gynecological symptoms such as irregular menstruation and red and leucorrhea; 3. Men's syndromes such as nocturnal emission and impotence.)
位置：在腰部，当后正中线上，第4腰椎棘突下凹陷中。 (On the waist, on the posterior median line, the fourth lumbar spinous process is sunken under the concave.)
